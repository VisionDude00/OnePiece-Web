### Additional Notes

Episode 112 serves as a crucial turning point where:
- The civil war becomes inevitable despite Vivi's best efforts
- The Straw Hats' individual battles are set up
- Themes of loyalty, sacrifice, and the tragedy of war are powerfully conveyed
- The X-mark symbol is established for future emotional payoff

#### Character Development
- **Vivi**: Shows her intelligence (detecting Mr. 2) and desperation (trying to stop war)
- **Karoo**: Displays ultimate loyalty and courage despite natural timidity
- **Luffy**: Demonstrates determination and characteristic stubbornness even when injured
- **Zoro**: Shows strategic thinking in devising the X-mark system
- **Mr. 2**: Reveals combat prowess and tactical use of Devil Fruit powers

#### Cultural Impact
The X-mark scene established here becomes one of One Piece's most iconic moments, often cited in discussions of the series' emotional peaks alongside the Merry farewell and Ace's death.
