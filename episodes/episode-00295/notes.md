# Research Notes for Episode 295

## Episode Overview
Episode 295 "Five Namis? Nami Strikes Back with Mirages!" is a pivotal episode in the Enies Lobby arc, focusing on Nami's battle against CP9 agent Kalifa. The episode showcases Nami's intelligence and tactical prowess as she discovers and exploits Kalifa's weakness.

## Key Findings

### Main Battle Focus
- This episode is one of the few major female vs female fights in One Piece
- Nami's victory demonstrates the series' theme of intelligence over brute strength
- The battle is interconnected with other ongoing fights in the Tower of Justice

### New Techniques Debuted
1. **Cool Charge** - Nami's preparatory technique for advanced weather attacks
2. **Mirage Tempo: Fata Morgana** - One of Nami's most iconic techniques, creating 5 illusory versions
3. **Shinaru Shigan "Whip"** - Kalifa's variation of the Rokushiki Shigan technique
4. **Makie** - Defensive technique used by Zoro and Sogeking

### Cultural References
- "Fata Morgana" is named after a complex mirage phenomenon
- The name comes from Morgan le Fay, a sorceress from Arthurian legend
- This shows Nami's scientific/meteorological approach to combat

### Production Details
- Episode aired January 28, 2007 in Japan
- 7.8% viewership rating, ranked 6th in time slot
- Opening: "Crazy Rainbow" by Tackey & Tsubasa
