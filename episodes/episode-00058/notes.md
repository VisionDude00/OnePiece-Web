## Devil Fruit Focus: Kama Kama no Mi

**Type:** Paramecia (anime-only)

**Abilities:**
- Permanently lengthened and sharpened fingernails
- Can create razor-sharp wind slashes
- Slashes can cut through solid rock and other hard materials
- Multiple simultaneous attacks possible
- Long-range combat capability

**Notable Techniques:**
1. **Kama Kama no Kamaitachi** - Single air blade
2. **Kama Kama no Tsumujikaze** - Multiple-hit whirlwind
3. **Kama Kama no Kamaitachi Midareuchi** - Ultimate multi-blade attack

**Comparison:** Similar to CP9's Rankyaku technique (kicks that create air blades)

## Canon Connections & Contradictions

### Connections
1. **Voice of All Things:** Luffy's ability to understand Ryu foreshadows the canonical "Voice of All Things" revealed in Zou arc (Episodes 750+)
2. **Oda's Approval:** Only filler arc that Eiichiro Oda publicly praised
3. **Original Film Concept:** Sennenryu plot was considered for theatrical film

### Contradictions
1. **Dragon Existence:** Later canon treats dragons as mythical/non-existent
   - Thriller Bark: Zoro doubts dragon existence
   - Punk Hazard (Ep 579+): Luffy and Zoro treat Vegapunk's dragon as unprecedented
2. **Continuity Issue:** Straw Hats act like they've never seen dragons despite meeting Ryu
