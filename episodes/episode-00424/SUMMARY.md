## Episode Summary

Luffy and Buggy crash into Level 1 of Impel Down, known as Crimson Hell, a deadly forest filled with razor-sharp blade trees and poisonous spiders. When Luffy shows Buggy an armband from his treasure, Buggy recognizes it as the legendary map to Captain John's treasure. In exchange for the armband, Buggy agrees to guide Luffy to Level 4 where Ace is imprisoned. After defeating Blue Gorilla guards, Luffy gives Buggy the armband upfront, showing his trust. However, Buggy's greed takes over and he attempts to escape, but a guard's attack causes both to fall through to Level 2.
