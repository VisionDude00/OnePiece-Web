# Episode 640 Research Notes

## Episode Overview
- **Title**: "Explore! Fairies' Island - Green Bit!" / "Adventure! The Island of Fairies, Green Bit"
- **Japanese**: 冒険!妖精の島グリーンビット (Bōken! Yōsei no Shima Gurīn Bitto)
- **Air Date**: April 13, 2014 (Japan), November 18, 2023 (US/Toonami)
- **Manga Chapters**: 710 (pages 14-19), 711 (pages 10-13), 712 (pages 2-7)

## Key Storylines

### Green Bit Exploration (Robin & Usopp)
- Group arrives at Green Bit, discovers crashed Marine ship
- Robin and Usopp scout the forest while Law takes Caesar to meeting point
- Marines attacked by "invisible" thieves (Tontattas moving too fast to see)
- Robin uses her Devil Fruit to capture the dwarves
- Leo appears and interrogates Marines
- When Marines don't lay down weapons, dwarves strip them to underwear

### Acacia/Dressrosa (Zoro & Wicca)
- Zoro running through port city with Wicca (the sword thief)
- Wicca injured (sprained ankle) from being caught
- She begs Zoro to take her to flower field
- Reveals urgent warning: Donquixote family plans to attack Thousand Sunny

### Dressrosa (Sanji & Violet)
- Sanji beaten and surrounded by Doflamingo's men
- Violet reveals she's an assassin for Doflamingo (Trébol Army)
- Uses Giro Giro no Mi to look into Sanji's mind
- Learns about Law-Luffy alliance but can't see details (Sanji's perverted thoughts)
- Sanji declares he believes her tears, never doubts woman's tears
- Violet touched by this, defeats her subordinates, saves Sanji
- Begins to reveal her true allegiance

## Character Debuts

### Leo
- Tontatta dwarf warrior
- First appearance asking Marines "good guys or bad guys?"
- Height: 23 cm
- Voice: Kōki Miyata (JP)
- Leader-type personality
- Demonstrates incredible speed and strength

### Wicca
- Tontatta dwarf scout/messenger
- Stole Zoro's sword
- Height: ~20 cm
- Voice: Rina Hidaka (JP)
- On urgent mission to warn about ship attack
- Shows determination despite injury

## Location Debuts

### Green Bit
- Island north of Dressrosa
- Connected by destroyed bridge (destroyed 200 years ago by Fighting Fish)
- Densely forested, lush vegetation
- Home to Tontatta Kingdom (underground)
- Chosen as meeting point for Law-Doflamingo exchange

## Important Reveals

1. **Tontatta Tribe Introduction**: First appearance of dwarves in One Piece anime
2. **Violet's True Nature**: Revealed as Doflamingo's assassin, then shows true allegiance
3. **Giro Giro no Mi Powers**: Can see through objects and into minds
4. **Donquixote Family's Plans**: Planning to attack the Thousand Sunny
5. **Green Bit's Nature**: Mysterious island with dangerous inhabitants and crashed Marines

## Themes
- **Trust vs. Deception**: Multiple layers of deception (Violet, "invisible" dwarves)
- **Size vs. Strength**: Tontattas demonstrate power despite tiny size
- **Chivalry**: Sanji's unwavering code about women's tears proves correct

## Production Notes
- **Opening**: "Wake Up!" by AAA (episodes 629-686)
- **Ending**: None (endings omitted from episode 279 onward)
- **Animation Director**: Kōhei Kureta
- **Animation Quality**: Standard for Dressrosa arc
