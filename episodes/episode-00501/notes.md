- **Gray Terminal Fire:** A devastating act of mass murder orchestrated by the Goa Kingdom nobility, representing one of the darkest moments in the series and a powerful allegory for class oppression.
- **Bluejam's Betrayal:** The nobles' betrayal of Bluejam shows that even those who serve corrupt systems are ultimately expendable.

- **Sabo:** Episodes shows his compassion, moral courage, and rejection of his noble heritage
- **Dragon:** Reveals he was born in Goa Kingdom, providing crucial backstory for his revolutionary motivations
- **Ace:** Demonstrates inherited Conqueror's Haki from Gol D. Roger
- **Bluejam:** Shows strong willpower by resisting Conqueror's Haki
