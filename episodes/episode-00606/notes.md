# Episode 606 Research Notes

## Episode Overview
Episode 606 "The Treacherous Vice Admiral! Demon Bamboo Vergo!" is a pivotal episode in the Punk Hazard Arc that reveals Vergo's betrayal and establishes him as a major antagonist.

## Key Observations

### Animation Quality
- Multiple reviews noted disappointment with the animation quality, particularly for key scenes like Sanji's entrance
- King Of Lightning's review specifically criticized the lack of impact in pivotal moments
- Fan discussions expressed frustration that "Sanji's Badass Entrance" was not well-executed

### Major Plot Revelations
1. **Vergo's Betrayal**: First major reveal that a Marine Vice Admiral is working for Doflamingo
2. **Joker = Doflamingo**: The connection between the underworld broker "Joker" and Warlord Doflamingo is revealed
3. **Rokushiki Mastery**: Vergo demonstrates advanced Marine combat techniques

### Character Moments
- **Sanji's "Heard Her Tears"**: One of the most iconic Sanji moments in the arc
- **Tashigi's Courage**: Despite being outmatched, shows her dedication to justice
- **Chopper's Medical Care**: Demonstrates his role as the crew's doctor in helping Mocha

### Manga Differences
- Anime added extended dragon attack scene
- Chopper and Mocha scene was expanded from the manga
- Episode covers Chapter 680 pages 2-19
