## Episode Overview
Episode 399: "Break Through the Siege! The Navy vs. the Three Captains" is a pivotal episode in the Sabaody Archipelago Arc that showcases the power and dynamics of three Supernova captains while introducing major elements that will impact the story going forward.

### Important Context

#### Opening Theme Change
- Episode 399 uses "Share the World" by Tohoshinki (TVXQ)
- This was Opening 11, celebrating One Piece's 10th anniversary
- First opening to feature Brook as an official Straw Hat crew member
- Episode range: 395-425

#### No Ending Theme
- One Piece stopped using ending themes from episode 279 to episode 1070
- Episode 399 falls within this gap, so has no ending song
- Extended opening theme used instead

#### Pacifista Significance
- This is the first time a complete Pacifista is clearly shown in the anime
- Characters (and audience) initially mistake it for the real Bartholomew Kuma
- This marks the beginning of the reveal of Dr. Vegapunk's Pacifista project
- The specific Pacifista in episode 399 is not identified by number (PX-1, PX-2, etc.)

#### Three Captains Dynamic
- The fan community began referring to Luffy, Law, and Kid as "The Three Captains" after this arc
- Theory: They would mirror Roger, Whitebeard, and Shiki from the previous generation
- This episode cements their rivalry and mutual respect
- Their promise to meet in the New World becomes a major throughline

### Episode Quality Notes
- Rated 9.0/10 on IMDb, one of the highest-rated episodes in the arc
- Animation quality: STANDARD (not sakuga, but solid)
- Animation Director: Masahiro Shimanuki (known for solid One Piece work)
- Episode Director: Yukihiko Nakao

### World-Building Impact
This episode contains several major revelations:

1. **Roger's Terminal Illness**: Rayleigh reveals Roger turned himself in because of incurable illness
2. **Roger's Willing Sacrifice**: He wasn't captured, he surrendered
3. **Pacifista Existence**: First clear evidence of the World Government's human weapon program
4. **Jean Bart's Freedom**: Shows Law's character and opposition to the World Government
5. **Supernova Alliance Potential**: Demonstrates these pirates can work together when needed

### Character Development

#### Eustass Kid
- First named technique (Punk Gibson)
- Establishes his brutal, overwhelming combat style
- Shows competitive but not completely hostile relationship with Luffy and Law

#### Trafalgar Law
- First clear Room demonstration
- Shows compassionate side by freeing Jean Bart
- Reveals tactical, surgical approach to combat
- Establishes him as both powerful and principled

#### Jean Bart
- Background as famous pirate captain before enslavement
- Carries the "Hoof of the Soaring Dragon" slave brand
- Deeply grateful to Law for freedom
- Will become important Heart Pirates member

#### Silvers Rayleigh
- Begins his role as historian/mentor
- Shares critical information about Roger
- Sets up future training arc potential
