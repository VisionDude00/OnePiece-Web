## Episode Synopsis
The Straw Hat Pirates depart from Skypiea with help from Pagaya and Conis, descending to the Blue Sea via Cloud End. As the Golden Bell rings in farewell, an Octopus Balloon cushions their fall. However, Luffy's antics cause the balloon to deflate, leading to a crash landing directly into Marine Base G-8.
