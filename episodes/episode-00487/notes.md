# Research Notes for Episode 487

## Episode Overview
Episode 487, titled "The Insatiable Akainu! Lava Fists Pummel Luffy!", is one of the most emotionally intense and pivotal episodes in the Marineford Arc. It features multiple critical moments that have lasting impacts throughout the series.

## Key Research Findings

### Primary Sources
- Episode aired February 13, 2011 in Japan (February 12, 2011 in US simulcast)
- Adapts Manga Chapter 578 "A Gift for the New Era" (pages 3-19)
- Received 11.1% viewership rating in Japan, ranking #3 for the week
- IMDb rating: 9.3/10

### Personal Observations
This episode marks one of the most critical turning points in One Piece. The moment Luffy receives his scar is etched into every fan's memory. The animation quality was enhanced for key moments, particularly Akainu's attacks and Law's dramatic entrance. The emotional weight of seeing so many characters sacrifice to save Luffy demonstrates the impact he's had on the world despite being a rookie pirate.

The episode masterfully balances multiple plotlines: Blackbeard's rise, the Marines' pyrrhic victory, Koby's awakening, and most importantly, the formation of future alliances that will carry through the New World saga.
