## Key Episode Moments

### Robin's Emotional Arc
- Episode 306 is NOT the "I want to live!" episode (that's Episode 278)
- This episode shows Robin's continued emotional development AFTER that declaration
- Key moment: Robin realizes "This Buster Call is different from Ohara because I'm no longer alone"
- This represents the fulfillment of Saul's promise that she would find friends

### Kokoro's Reveal
- First confirmed mermaid encounter for the Straw Hats
- Comedic shock value: crew nearly dies from shock at her appearance
- Important world-building: explains mermaid tail fins split at age 30
- Later callbacks: Straw Hats pretend Camie is their first mermaid

### Franky's Character Moment
- Poetic justice: punches the son of the man who killed Tom
- Direct inspiration for Robin's breakthrough
- Cements his role as future Straw Hat member
