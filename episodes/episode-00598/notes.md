## Episode Overview
Episode 598: "A Samurai Who Can Cut Fire! Foxfire Kin'emon!" is a pivotal episode in the Punk Hazard arc that formally introduces Kin'emon as a complete character and plants seeds for the massive Wano Country arc that comes later.

## Key Findings

### Major Story Beats
1. **Vergo's Dominance**: The episode powerfully establishes Vergo as a major threat by showing him brutally defeating Law using his literal heart. This creates genuine stakes and demonstrates the consequences of Law's deal with Caesar.

2. **Kin'emon's Complete Introduction**: After several episodes of the character being separated into parts (a comedic but intriguing mystery), this episode finally reunites him and reveals his true identity, abilities, and mission.

3. **First Wano References**: This is one of the first explicit mentions of Wano Country in the anime, setting up what will become a multi-year arc storyline.

### Foreshadowing and Callbacks
- Zoro's interest in the Foxfire Style directly foreshadows him learning fire-cutting techniques used in the Wano arc (episodes 1000+)
- Momonosuke is mentioned but not yet seen, building anticipation
- The time travel element is hinted at but not revealed (confirmed in episode 909)
- Vergo's connection to the Donquixote Pirates sets up the Dressrosa arc
