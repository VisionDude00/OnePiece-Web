# Research Notes for Episode 133

## Episode Overview
Episode 133 "A Recipe Handed Down! Sanji, the Iron Man of Curry!" is a filler episode that aired on November 17, 2002 in Japan. It's part of the Post-Arabasta Arc (episodes 131-135), where each episode focuses on a different Straw Hat crew member.

## Key Research Findings

### Production Notes
- This is the **first episode** to feature "Free Will" by Ruppina as the ending theme
- The opening "Hikari e" by The Babystars was already in use (episodes 116-168)
- Episode received a 6.7/10 rating on IMDb
- Documented animation error: A Marine officer depicted with two right arms
- Screenplay by Junki Takegami, directed by Hidehiko Kadota

### Character Development
- **Sanji**: First time taking on a true mentor role, mirroring Zeff's teaching methods
- **Tajio**: New character who shares Sanji's dream of finding All Blue
- **Robin**: Shows integration with crew by rescuing Tajio despite him being a Marine

### Thematic Elements
1. **Mentorship and Legacy**: Sanji passes on knowledge learned from Zeff
2. **Dreams Transcending Boundaries**: Pirate and Marine connect through shared dream
3. **Patience and Dedication**: Emphasis on proper technique over rushing

## Cultural Context

### Japanese Title Meaning
The Japanese title uses parentheses to show "Recipe" (レシピ) is read as "Dream" (Yume), emphasizing the dual meaning of inherited dreams and recipes. This is a clever wordplay highlighting the episode's theme.

### Cooking Philosophy
The episode reflects traditional Japanese apprenticeship methods where students learn through observation and practice rather than explicit instruction. This mirrors real culinary training in Japan.
