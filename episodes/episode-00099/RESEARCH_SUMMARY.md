## Synopsis
The Straw Hat Pirates arrive at the desert village of Ido, where they discover fake Rebel Army soldiers led by Camus protecting the town. Princess Vivi decides to test whether these imposters have genuine courage by having Luffy challenge them. Camus must choose between running away or standing up to protect the children who look up to him as a hero. Through his decision to fight despite his fear, Camus transforms from a selfish conman into a true protector of the village.
