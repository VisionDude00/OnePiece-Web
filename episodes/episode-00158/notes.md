## Episode Overview

## Key Findings

### Major Events
1. **Conis's Confession**: This is Conis's defining character moment where she chooses morality over self-preservation by confessing the trap to the Straw Hats despite knowing it will result in her execution.

2. **El Thor Debut**: First on-screen use of Enel's signature technique El Thor (Heaven's Judgment), which becomes his primary method of executing divine punishment throughout the arc.

3. **Mantra Demonstration**: Episode clearly demonstrates how Enel's Mantra (Observation Haki) is enhanced by his Goro Goro no Mi, allowing omniscient surveillance across all of Skypiea.

4. **Gan Fall's Revelation**: Gan Fall is revealed as the former God of Skypiea, establishing him as the legitimate ruler who was overthrown by Enel.
