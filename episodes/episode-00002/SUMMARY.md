## Significance
Episode 2 is arguably one of the most important episodes in One Piece history. It:
- Introduces Zoro, the first crew member and future right-hand man
- Establishes the recruitment pattern for future crew members
- Introduces corrupt Marines (Morgan/Helmeppo)
- Shows Luffy's willingness to oppose corrupt authority
- Demonstrates the mutual respect between Luffy and Zoro
- Begins the formation of the Straw Hat Pirates as a crew and family

The episode sets the foundation for the entire series by showing that:
1. Crew members join on their own terms with their own dreams
2. Not all authority figures are heroic or just
3. Luffy judges people by their actions, not their reputations
4. Dreams and determination matter more than current circumstances
