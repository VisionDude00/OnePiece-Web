## Notable Findings

### Devil Fruit Comparison
The Atsu Atsu no Mi (filler) has interesting similarities to Charlotte Oven's Netsu Netsu no Mi (canon):
- Both are heat-based Paramecia fruits
- Both allow extreme temperature manipulation
- Atsu Atsu reaches 10,000 degrees; Netsu Netsu's max unknown
- This parallel noted in trivia section

### Gear 2 in Filler
This episode is notable for featuring Gear 2 in a filler context, showing how the technique had become integrated into Luffy's standard fighting style by this point in the anime (post-Enies Lobby).

### Ice Hunter Arc Context
- Episodes 326-335 (10 episodes)
- Generally considered one of the weaker filler arcs
- Episode 334 is the penultimate episode of the arc
- Arc takes place between Water 7 and Thriller Bark in anime continuity
