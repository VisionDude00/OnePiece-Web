# Research Notes for Episode 540

## Research Date
December 23, 2025

## Episode Overview
Episode 540 "A Hero Who Freed the Slaves! An Adventurer Tiger!" is a flashback episode that takes place 16 years before the current timeline. It's a pivotal episode that introduces two of the most important figures in Fish-Man Island history: Queen Otohime and Fisher Tiger.

## Key Findings

### Thematic Richness
This episode is exceptionally rich in themes, addressing:
- Slavery and liberation
- Racism and prejudice
- Different approaches to achieving equality (peaceful vs. militant)
- Compassion and understanding
- The cost of standing up for principles

The episode has been widely praised for its mature handling of these real-world issues.

### Historical Parallels
Multiple sources confirm the intentional parallels to civil rights leaders:
- **Fisher Tiger = Malcolm X**: Militant approach, direct action, some separatist tendencies
- **Queen Otohime = MLK**: Peaceful approach, integration, changing hearts through understanding

Some sources also mention Nat Turner as a possible inspiration for Fisher Tiger's character.

### Character Debuts
This episode debuts several important characters:
- Queen Otohime (critical importance)
- Fisher Tiger (critical importance)
- Papaneel (minor character)
- Aladine (Sun Pirates member)
- Zururin (mentioned but minimal detail found)

- Air Date: March 25, 2012 (Japan), March 24, 2012 (US Simulcast)
- TV Rating: 8.8% (Ranked 7th)
