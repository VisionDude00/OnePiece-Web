## Synopsis
Luffy defeats Arlong in an epic final battle, destroying Arlong Park and freeing Cocoyasi Village from eight years of oppression. After discovering Nami's blood-covered pen, Luffy destroys the cartography room where she was imprisoned, symbolically freeing her from her past. Using his new technique Gomu Gomu no Ono, Luffy sends Arlong crashing through five floors, collapsing the entire building. Standing atop the ruins, Luffy declares Nami his friend and crewmate, and she officially joins the Straw Hat Pirates as their navigator. Meanwhile, corrupt Marine Captain Nezumi reports Luffy to headquarters, setting in motion his first bounty.
