1. **Voice Acting Detail**: Yuriko Yamaguchi voices both Nico Robin and Nico Olvia in Japanese, while Stephanie Young voices both in English. This creates a beautiful connection between mother and daughter through voice.

This episode explores several profound themes:
- **Maternal Sacrifice**: Olvia's decision to stay away from Robin to protect her
- **Knowledge as Dangerous**: The World Government treating historical research as a capital crime
- **The Cost of Truth**: Scholars facing death for seeking historical truth
