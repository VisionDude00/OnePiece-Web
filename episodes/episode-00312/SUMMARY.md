## Synopsis
The Straw Hat Pirates return from Enies Lobby aboard the damaged Going Merry. When Iceburg reveals the ship's keel is irreparably broken, the crew holds a Viking funeral to honor their beloved first vessel. As snow falls and memories flash by, the Merry's spirit speaks one final time, thanking the crew and apologizing for not being able to carry them further.
