# Research Notes for Episode 269

## Episode Overview
Episode 269 "Robin Betrayed! The Motive of the World Government!" aired as part of a television special with episode 268 on June 25, 2006. This episode is a crucial turning point in the Enies Lobby arc, revealing the true nature of CP9 and setting up the climactic battles.

## Key Findings

### Character Development
- **Rob Lucci**: This episode contains one of the most important character reveals for Lucci, where he openly states his desire for "government-sanctioned killing." This establishes him as a psychopath rather than a dutiful agent.
- **Robin**: Her trauma from the Ohara incident is reinforced through the brief flashback when the Buster Call is mentioned. This foreshadows the full flashback arc coming in episodes 275-278.
- **Spandam**: His insecurity and cruelty are on full display when he beats Robin for questioning his authority.

### Thematic Significance
This episode explores the theme of institutional corruption, showing how the World Government's "justice" is really a cover for bloodlust (Lucci) and personal ambition (Spandam). The broken agreement with Robin symbolizes how the powerful manipulate rules to serve their interests.
