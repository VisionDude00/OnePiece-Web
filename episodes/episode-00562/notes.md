### Key Findings

#### 1. Technique Debuts
This episode features TWO technique debuts:
- **Umidaiko (Ocean Drum)** - Hody's Fish-Man Karate technique
- **Ultramarine** - Fukaboshi's Merman Combat technique
- **Haki-Imbued Jet Pistol** - Luffy combining Gear Second with Haki

#### 2. Important Haki Revelation
Luffy explicitly states his Busoshoku Haki can't yet fully protect against piercing attacks (only blunt attacks). This is significant character development showing his limitations post-timeskip.

#### 3. Joy Boy Lore
Episode 562 contains crucial world-building about:
- Noah's true purpose (mass migration to surface)
- Joy Boy's broken promise
- Connection to Poseidon (Shirahoshi)
- The Poneglyph apology in the Sea Forest

#### 4. Energy Steroids Transformation
Hody's transformation in this episode is visually dramatic:
- Massive muscle growth
- Complete hair whitening
- Size increase
- Later consequences: rapid aging and physical deterioration

#### 5. Thematic Elements
The episode sets up the revelation that Hody's hatred is inherited prejudice, not personal tragedy - a major thematic statement by Oda about cyclical hatred.

## Interesting Trivia Discovered

1. **Name Etymology:** "Ultramarine" comes from Latin "ultramarinus" meaning "beyond the sea" - perfect for a merman technique!

2. **Amateur Karate:** Despite Hody's impressive power from Energy Steroids, both Luffy and Jinbe later note his Fish-Man Karate is at an amateur level.

3. **Ammo Knights:** The name is a pun on "Ammonite" (fossilized snails), which their spiral shell armor resembles.

4. **Critical Reception:** The Luffy vs. Hody fight is considered one of the most unbalanced fights in One Piece, serving more to showcase post-timeskip abilities than provide tension.
