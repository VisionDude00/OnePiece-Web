## Synopsis
Inside the Rainbow Mist, Henzo and the Straw Hats struggle to find a way out of the mysterious ship graveyard known as Ape's Concert. Henzo desperately tries to reconnect with his childhood friends, the Pumpkin Pirates, who don't recognize him after 50 years of separation. Meanwhile, the crew discovers that space itself is distorted within the Rainbow Mist, creating closed loops that make escape nearly impossible. Outside the mist, Nami outsmarts Wetton's men to obtain a safety device, setting up a dangerous journey to reunite with her crewmates.
