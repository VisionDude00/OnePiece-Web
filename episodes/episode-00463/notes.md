## Key Findings

### Major Reveals in This Episode

1. **Akainu's Devil Fruit Powers (CRITICAL)**
   - First time Magu Magu no Mi is shown in the anime
   - Demonstrates Dai Funka technique
   - Creates volcanic meteor shower
   - Establishes Akainu as one of the most dangerous combatants
   - Note: The name "Magu Magu no Mi" is not revealed until later (SBS Volume 62)

2. **Little Oars Jr. Debut (MAJOR)**
   - First appearance in Episode 463 / Chapter 554
   - 60 meters tall (3x normal giant size)
   - Captain of Little Pirates
   - Subordinate ally of Whitebeard Pirates
   - Friendship with Ace established as motivation

3. **Techniques Showcased**
   - Dai Funka (Akainu) - First use
   - Yasakani no Magatama (Kizaru) - May have appeared earlier
   - Fujiazami (Marco) - Defensive phoenix technique

**Ending Song:** None
