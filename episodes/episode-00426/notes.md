## Important Findings

### Canon vs Non-Canon Status
**CRITICAL NOTE:** This episode is part of the Little East Blue arc, which is **anime-original filler** created to promote One Piece Film: Strong World. However:
- **Shiki (character)** is CANON - appears in Chapter 0 and mentioned in Chapter 530
- **Fuwa Fuwa no Mi (Devil Fruit)** is CANON - confirmed by Oda in SBS Volume 109
- **Little East Blue arc events** are NON-CANON
- **Strong World movie events** are NON-CANON

### Opening Song Debut
- Episode 426 marks the debut of "Kaze wo Sagashite" (Search for the Wind)
- Performed by Mari Yaguchi with Straw Hat
- Used for episodes 426-458
- Unique feature: No opening narration in this episode
- Initial broadcasts (episodes 426-429) didn't credit the singer; corrected on DVD

### No Ending Theme
- Episode 426 falls within the period where One Piece stopped airing ending themes (Episode 279-1070)
- This was done to allow for longer opening sequences

### Shiki's Historical Significance
- First and only prisoner to escape Impel Down before Luffy's mass breakout
- His escape hurt Magellan's pride and the prison's reputation
- Magellan was vice warden at the time (later became chief warden)
- Shiki's escape occurred approximately 20 years before the episode's present-day events

### Fuwa Fuwa no Mi Trivia
- Oda originally didn't want a flight-based ability in the main story (thought it didn't fit pirates)
- Created the fruit for Strong World movie
- Made it canon because Shiki himself is canon
- Considered one of the most powerful Paramecia-type Devil Fruits
