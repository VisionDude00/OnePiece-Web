## Interesting Observations

1. **Davy Back Fight Consequences:** This arc explores the lasting impact of Davy Back Fight losses, showing how crew members gained through the game can cause internal conflicts.

2. **Luffy's Compassion:** Despite Foxy being an antagonist, Luffy rescues him from the storm, showing his consistent compassion.

3. **The Championship Afro Returns:** Callback to episodes 214-216 where Luffy wore an afro for the boxing match.

4. **Aokiji's Independence:** His unauthorized departure from Marine Headquarters foreshadows his "Lazy Justice" philosophy and eventual departure from the Marines.

5. **HD Broadcast:** Episode 225 was broadcast in 16:9 HDTV format, as One Piece had transitioned to HD from episode 207.
