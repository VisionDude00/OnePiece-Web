## Key Findings

### Character Debuts
Three characters make their anime debut in this episode:
1. **Whitey Bay** - The Ice Witch, captain of an icebreaker ship and allied to Whitebeard
2. **Bastille** - Marine Vice Admiral, "Shark Cutter"
3. **Dalmatian** - Marine Vice Admiral with dalmatian Zoan powers

**NOTE:** Onigumo appears in this episode but debuted much earlier (Episode 302). He's not included in the character debuts.

### Devil Fruit Showcases
While the Gura Gura no Mi and Hie Hie no Mi debuted in earlier episodes, Episode 462 features major new ability reveals:
- **Gura Gura no Mi:** Tsunami creation and atmospheric cracking
- **Hie Hie no Mi:** Ice Age technique (large-scale ocean freezing)
- **Dalmatian's Zoan:** First appearance of the unnamed dalmatian Zoan fruit

### Major Events
13 significant events documented, including:
- Whitebeard's demonstration of "world-destroying" power
- Aokiji vs Whitebeard confrontation
- Creation of the frozen battlefield
- Mihawk's decision to attack Whitebeard
- Ace's flashback and apology
- Luffy's approach to Marineford

### Cultural Impact
The episode contains the scene (around 14:24) that became the source of the famous "The One Piece is Real" Whitebeard meme, though the widely circulated version appears to be an edited/zoomed version of the actual shot.
