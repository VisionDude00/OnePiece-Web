# Research Notes for Episode 442

## Research Date
December 12, 2025

## Episode Overview
Episode 442: "Ace's Convoy Begins! Battle on the Lowest Floor - Level 6!" is a pivotal episode in the Impel Down arc where Luffy reaches Level 6 only to discover Ace has already been transferred to Marine custody.

## Key Research Findings

### Episode Information
- **Japanese Air Date**: March 14, 2010
- **US DVD Release**: January 26, 2016
- **TV Rating**: 12.0% (Rank 3 for the week in Japan)
- **Adapts**: Manga Chapter 540 (pages 2-15)
- **Opening**: "Kaze wo Sagashite" by Mari Yaguchi with Straw Hat (Opening 12, not 13 as initially thought)
- **Ending**: None (endings were discontinued from Episode 279-1071)

### Character Debut Notes
- **Jinbe's Voice Actor**: This episode features Katsuhisa Hoki as Jinbe's voice actor. He replaced the late Daisuke Gori starting from Episode 440. Episode 442 is one of his early performances as Jinbe.
- **First Major Interaction**: While Jinbe debuted in Episode 430, Episode 442 is his first major speaking role and interaction with Luffy.

### Devil Fruit Demonstration
- **Choki Choki no Mi**: Episode 442 features the most comprehensive demonstration of Inazuma's Devil Fruit powers to date
- The ability to cut solid stone as if it were paper and fold it into barriers is showcased
- Ivankov explicitly explains the fruit's name and Inazuma's status as a "Scissors Human"

### Major Plot Points
1. **Empty Cell Discovery**: The devastating moment when Luffy discovers Ace's empty cell
2. **Hannyabal's Trap**: Well-executed plan with sleeping gas, destroyed elevators, and blocked stairs
3. **Ace's Rebellion**: Shows Ace's love for Luffy but also his complete weakness
4. **Transfer to Onigumo**: The official point of no return for Ace's execution
5. **Crocodile Encounter**: Shocking return of a major antagonist
6. **Ivankov's Secret**: Introduction of the mysterious "weakness" Ivankov knows about Crocodile

### Mysteries Introduced
The episode introduces one of One Piece's enduring mysteries: what is Crocodile's "precious weakness" that Ivankov knows about? As of 2025, this remains unresolved in the series.

### Fan Theory Research
The Crocodile secret theory is by far the most discussed aspect of this episode:
1. **Most Popular**: Crocodile was originally female and Ivankov changed their sex with the Horm-Horm Fruit
2. **Alternative**: Crocodile was a Celestial Dragon's slave
3. Both theories remain unconfirmed as of 2025
