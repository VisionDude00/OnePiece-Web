## Synopsis
Luffy, Ivankov, and Inazuma reach Level 6 of Impel Down only to discover Ace has already been transferred to Marine custody. They meet Jinbe, Ace's former cellmate, who urgently informs them of Ace's recent departure. Hannyabal traps them with sleeping gas, but Inazuma uses his Choki Choki no Mi ability to save them. Meanwhile, Ace is handed over to Vice Admiral Onigumo for transport to Marineford. The episode ends with Luffy encountering his former enemy Crocodile, who offers to help them escape in exchange for his freedom.

## Significance to One Piece Story
Episode 442 represents a crucial turning point in the Impel Down arc. Luffy's failure to rescue Ace from the prison sets the stage for the even more dangerous Marineford War arc. The formation of the Prison Escape Team with Jinbe and Crocodile creates unlikely alliances that will play major roles in future events. This episode also plants seeds for Jinbe's eventual recruitment to the Straw Hat Pirates and introduces mysteries about Crocodile that remain unresolved years later.
