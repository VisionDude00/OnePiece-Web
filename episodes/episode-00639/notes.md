- **Jean Ango**: Bounty hunter known as "Jean the Bandit" with unique weapon-throwing style
- **Ucy**: Fighting bull gladiator, later befriended by Luffy

#### Location Debuts
- **Green Bit**: Uninhabited island north of Dressrosa, home to Tontatta Kingdom underground
- **Iron Bridge**: 200-year-closed bridge connecting Dressrosa to Green Bit

#### Species Debut
- **Fighting Fish**: Massive aggressive fish with bull-like heads that have made travel to Green Bit impossible for 200 years

#### Major Character Moments
1. **Bellamy's Growth**: Acknowledges Luffy's Haki development and admits Luffy's support crushed his pride

#### Techniques Shown
- **Robin**: Mil Fleur: Gigantesco Mano Spank (against Fighting Fish)
- **Sanji**: Amour Shot (against Doflamingo's operatives)

## Episode Significance

### Narrative Function
This episode serves as a transitional episode that:
1. Advances multiple storylines simultaneously (Colosseum, Green Bit journey, Sanji subplot)
2. Introduces Block C fighters who will later form parts of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet
3. Establishes key relationships (Bartolomeo-Luffy connection, Violet as ally)
4. Creates tension with the approaching Caesar-Doflamingo exchange

### Thematic Elements
- **Trust vs. Betrayal**: Explored through Sanji-Violet relationship and Law's precautions with Caesar
- **Pride and Growth**: Bellamy's character development shows the impact of kindness over strength
- **Obstacles and Perseverance**: The Fighting Fish literally represent barriers that must be overcome

### Animation Quality

- Dressrosa arc known for pacing issues and budget constraints
- Fighting Fish attack scenes were extended from manga for dramatic effect
