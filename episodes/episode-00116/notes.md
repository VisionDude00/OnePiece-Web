# Research Notes for Episode 116

## Episode Overview
**Title:** "Transformed into Nami! Bon Clay's Rapid-Fire Ballet Kenpo!"
**Air Date:** June 23, 2002 (Japan)
**Arc:** Arabasta Arc

## Key Research Findings

### Episode Significance
This episode is particularly notable for:
1. **Opening Theme Debut**: First episode to use "Hikari e" by The Babystars, which contains major spoilers by showing Nico Robin and Chopper as crew members before their official joining.
2. **Sanji Character Development**: One of the most important episodes for establishing Sanji's chivalry as both a strength and critical weakness.
3. **Fight Choreography**: Widely praised as one of the best-animated fights in the Arabasta Arc, particularly the handstand kick exchange sequence.

### Manga Correspondence
Episode 116 adapts portions of three manga chapters:
- Chapter 188 (pages 4-19): "Okama Kenpo"
- Chapter 189 (pages 2-19): "2"
- Chapter 190 (pages 2-10)

### Voice Acting Note
Interesting trivia: Kazuki Yao voices both Mr. 2 Bon Clay AND Franky in the Japanese version, though the English dub uses different actors (Barry Yandell for Bon Clay, Patrick Seitz for Franky).

### Critical Story Elements

#### Sanji's Weakness Established
This episode clearly establishes that Sanji cannot attack anyone who appears female, regardless of their actual gender. This becomes a recurring theme:
- Episode 116: Mr. 2 transforms into Nami, Sanji paralyzed
- Episode 274 (Enies Lobby): Sanji cannot fight Kalifa, leading to defeat
- Sets up long-term character consistency

#### Mane Mane no Mi Mechanics Revealed
Key discoveries about the Clone-Clone Fruit:
1. **Activation**: Touch target's face with right hand to copy
2. **Reversal**: Touch own face with left hand to revert
3. **Combat Limitation**: Cannot use full Okama Kenpo while transformed (must revert for proper technique execution)
4. **Psychological Warfare**: Can be used to exploit emotional attachments

### Plot Threads
Multiple storylines converge in this episode:
1. **Main Focus**: Sanji defeats Mr. 2
2. **Palace Storyline**: Vivi plans destruction, Crocodile arrives
3. **Setup for Next Battles**: Nami pursued by Mr. 1 and Miss Doublefinger
4. **Chopper/Usopp**: Mentioned in recap of their victory

### Thematic Analysis

#### Honor and Respect
Despite being enemies, Sanji shows respect to Mr. 2 after defeating him ("Thanks for the good fight"). This moment:
- Establishes mutual respect
- Foreshadows Mr. 2's later alliance with the Straw Hats
- Demonstrates One Piece's theme that honor transcends allegiances

#### Sacrifice and Leadership
Vivi's willingness to destroy the palace shows her growth as a leader willing to make difficult decisions for the greater good.

#### Intelligence vs. Strength
Both fighters demonstrate that tactical thinking (Sanji discovering the weakness, Mr. 2 exploiting Sanji's chivalry) can be more decisive than raw power.
