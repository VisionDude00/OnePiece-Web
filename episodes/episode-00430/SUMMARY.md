## Key Highlights

### Major Character Debut
**Jinbe** makes his anime debut in this episode, revealed as the imprisoned Warlord in Level 6 alongside Ace. His introduction establishes:
- His principled refusal to fight Whitebeard despite losing his Warlord title
- His deep debt to Whitebeard for protecting Fish-Man Island from slavers
- His honorable character and loyalty

**Historical Note:** This episode featured the late Daisuke Gōri's voice acting as Jinbe (episodes 430-432), one of his final roles before his passing in January 2010.

### Story Progression
**Level 2 (Beast Hell):**
- Luffy, Buggy, and Galdino battle through dangerous beasts
- Encounter with the Sphinx guardian who speaks only in noodle names
- Floor collapse leads to descent into Level 3 (Starvation Hell)

**Level 6 (Eternal Hell):**
- Jinbe explains Whitebeard's protection of Fish-Man Island
- Crocodile and other prisoners express desire to kill Whitebeard
- Information revealed about Level 6's purpose - housing criminals erased from history

### Character Debuts
1. **Jinbe** - Warlord of the Sea, fish-man, future Straw Hat member
2. **Sphinx** - Level 2 guardian with noodle-speaking quirk
3. **Doha Ittanka II** - Level 6 prisoner
