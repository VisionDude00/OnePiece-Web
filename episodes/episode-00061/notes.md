# Episode 61 Research Notes

## Research Summary

**Episode:** 61 - "Angry Finale! Cross the Red Line!"
**Research Date:** 2025-11-18
**Status:** Complete

### Key Findings

This episode serves as the finale of the Warship Island Arc (the first filler arc in One Piece) and marks a pivotal transition point as the Straw Hats enter the Grand Line.

### Major Events Documented

1. **Eric's Betrayal** - Eric attacks Nelson, revealing his true villainous nature
2. **Ultimate Technique Debut** - Kama Kama no Kamaitachi Midareuchi showcased
3. **Eric's Defeat** - Luffy defeats Eric with Gomu Gomu techniques
4. **Nami's Clever Victory** - Nami tricks Eric into falling into the sea
5. **Reverse Mountain Debut** - First appearance of this crucial location
6. **Apis's Farewell** - Apis chooses to stay and protect Warship Island
7. **Grand Line Entry** - The Straw Hats successfully cross Reverse Mountain

### Notable Aspects

- **Filler Arc Praise**: This is one of the only filler arcs that Eiichiro Oda publicly praised
- **Final Ending Theme**: Last episode to use "RUN! RUN! RUN!" by Maki Otsuki
- **4Kids Changes**: Episodes 61, 62, and 64 were combined into a single episode in the 4Kids dub
- **Canon Integration**: The second half of the episode (Reverse Mountain) adapts manga Chapter 101

### Character Developments

- **Apis**: Makes mature decision to stay as guardian of Dragon's Nest
- **Eric**: Revealed as primary antagonist, ultimately defeated
- **Nami**: Demonstrates tactical intelligence and navigation expertise
- **Luffy**: Shows growth as a fighter, defeats arc's main antagonist
