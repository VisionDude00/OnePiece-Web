# Research Notes for Episode 209

## Research Date
2025-11-18

## Episode Overview
Episode 209: "Round 1! One Lap of the Donut Race!" is the first competitive episode of the Davy Back Fight arc, where the Straw Hats and Foxy Pirates compete in a barrel boat race around Long Ring Long Land.

## Key Findings

### Character Debuts
- **Itomimizu**: Announcer and referee for the Davy Back Fight, provides commentary from his bird Chuchun
- **Chuchun**: Giant Super Sparrow that serves as Itomimizu's mount
- **Capote**: Billfish fish-man who uses Fish-Man Karate and combines with Monda for the Two-Fish Engine
- **Monda**: Star Shark that pulls the Cutie Wagon and combines with Capote

### New Techniques
- **Kaimen Wari** (Ocean Surface Splitter): Capote's Fish-Man Karate technique
- **Ocho Fleur: Flip**: Robin's Hana Hana no Mi technique to flip opponents
- **Two-Fish Engine**: Combination technique between Capote and Monda

### Manga Differences
The anime significantly expanded this episode:
- Manga: Only Nami, Usopp, and Robin participate in the race
- Anime: The entire Straw Hat crew participates with multiple boats
- Anime added more Foxy Pirates crew members and racing teams

### Production Notes
- First widescreen opening for One Piece (Kokoro no Chizu)
- BOYSTYLE band members who sang the opening also voiced the four female racers
- Episode adapts Chapter 306 (p. 16-19) and Chapter 307 (p. 2-19)
