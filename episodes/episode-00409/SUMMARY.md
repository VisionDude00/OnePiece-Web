## Synopsis
Luffy awakens naked in a prison cell on Amazon Lily and is examined by Kuja women who have never seen a man before. When Kikyo attempts to execute him, he escapes by punching through the roof. He takes Marguerite hostage to retrieve his precious Vivre Card, which will help him find his way back to his scattered crew.
