### G-8 Arc Context

The G-8 Arc is widely considered the **best filler arc in One Piece** and possibly one of the best filler arcs in all of anime. Key reasons:
- Seamless integration with canon storyline
- Compelling antagonist (Vice Admiral Jonathan)
- Character-driven humor and moments
- Doesn't contradict canon
- Features strategic rather than pure combat focus
- Contains canon material from Chapter 303

### Vice Admiral Jonathan

Jonathan is an exceptional anime-only character who:
- Serves as main antagonist without direct combat
- Uses strategy and chess-like planning
- Shows a different side of the Marines (competent but compassionate)
- Is married to Jessica (base head chef)
- Is allegedly Akainu's protégé despite different philosophies
- Never directly fights Luffy (rare for antagonists)
- Has become a fan-favorite despite being non-canon
