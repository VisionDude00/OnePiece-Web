### 2. Key Discoveries

#### Character Debut: Funkfreed
- First appearance of Funkfreed, Spandam's sword that has consumed the Zou Zou no Mi
- This marks the debut of the Elephant-Elephant Fruit in the anime
- Notable as one of the few inanimate objects with Devil Fruit powers, showcasing Vegapunk's technology
- Full abilities not revealed until Episode 285

#### Voice Actor Change
- Episode 254 is the first episode where Kazue Ikura voices Tony Tony Chopper
- Ikue Otani went on maternity leave, with Ikura serving as temporary replacement for episodes 254-263
- Ikura would later voice Marine Captain Sentomaru
- This is a significant production note worth documenting

#### Ending Song Significance
- "Dear Friends" by TRIPLANE appears for the last time in Episode 255
- The song is dedicated to the Going Merry and later plays during Merry's funeral in Episode 312
- Episode 254 is the penultimate episode featuring this emotionally significant ending
