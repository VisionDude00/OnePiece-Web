# Episode 385 - Arriving at Halfway Through the Grand Line! The Red Line

## Quick Facts
- **Air Date (Japan)**: January 18, 2009
- **Air Date (US)**: July 14, 2015
- **Arc**: Sabaody Archipelago Arc (First Episode)
- **Saga**: Summit War Saga
- **Animation Quality**: Standard

## What Happens

The Straw Hat Pirates reach the Red Line, marking the halfway point of the Grand Line. While reflecting on their journey and searching for a route to Fish-Man Island using the Shark Submerge III, Luffy, Robin, and Brook are attacked by a Sea Rabbit. After Luffy defeats it, the creature spits out two unexpected passengers: Camie, a friendly mermaid, and Pappag, a talking starfish fashion designer.

Meanwhile, at Marine Headquarters, Fleet Admiral Sengoku confronts Bartholomew Kuma about allowing the Straw Hats to escape, while Vice Admiral Garp defends his grandson Luffy's tenacity.

## Major Debuts

### Characters
- **Camie**: A 16-year-old kissing gourami mermaid who dreams of becoming a fashion designer
- **Pappag**: A talking starfish and creator of the "Criminal" fashion brand

### Locations
- **Red Line**: The massive continent dividing the world, marking the Grand Line's halfway point

## Significance

Episode 385 marks the beginning of the critically acclaimed Sabaody Archipelago Arc. It serves as both a milestone moment for the crew (reaching the Grand Line's midpoint) and the introduction of key characters who will lead them into the arc's major conflicts. The episode balances reflection on the crew's journey with setup for the dramatic events to come.

## Key Moments

1. **Marine Leadership Tension** (02:30): Sengoku confronts Kuma about sparing the Straw Hats
2. **Red Line Arrival** (05:15): The crew reaches the halfway point of the Grand Line
3. **Shark Submerge III Deployment** (11:20): First major use of Franky's submarine
4. **Sea Rabbit Battle** (14:30-15:45): Luffy defeats the creature attacking their submarine
5. **Camie's Debut** (16:30): The mermaid is rescued from the Sea Rabbit
6. **Pappag's Debut** (17:15): The talking starfish introduces himself
7. **Missing Friend Setup** (21:30): Camie mentions her missing friend, foreshadowing the next phase

## Themes
- Journey and Progress
- Unexpected Encounters and Friendships
- Dreams and Ambition
- Reflection and Forward Momentum

## Trivia

- This episode uses the 10th anniversary remix of "We Are!" by Tohoshinki (TVXQ)
- Camie previously appeared in the manga's cover story "Hatchan's Sea-Floor Stroll" before her anime debut
- Episode 385 has no ending theme, as One Piece episodes 279-1070 used extended openings instead
- The episode adapts only pages 7-18 of Chapter 490, with the rest split between Episodes 381 and 386

## Fan Theories Sparked

The episode's focus on the Red Line reinvigorated fan theories about:
- The Red Line being artificially created by the World Government
- The endgame involving destroying the Red Line to accomplish multiple Straw Hat dreams
- The Red Line's connection to Norse mythology and world-building

## Reception

The episode is viewed positively as a strong setup episode that:
- Provides a satisfying milestone moment
- Introduces likeable new characters
- Naturally transitions from Thriller Bark to Sabaody
- Maintains the series' adventurous spirit before the arc's darker turn

## What's Next

The rescue of Camie and Pappag sets up the crew's mission to help find Camie's missing friend, which will lead them into conflict with the Flying Fish Riders and eventually to Sabaody Archipelago, where they'll encounter the Eleven Supernovas and face one of their greatest challenges yet.
