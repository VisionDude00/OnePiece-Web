### Opening/Ending Theme Clarification
**Important Discovery**: Episode 489 has NO ending theme. Toei Animation stopped airing ending themes from Episode 279 to Episode 1070 to allow for longer openings. This is a common pattern during this era of One Piece.

- **Opening**: "One day" by The ROOTLESS (episodes 459-492)
- **Ending**: None (standard practice for this period)
