# Research Notes for Episode 159

## Episode Overview
Episode 159 "Onward Crow! To the Sacrificial Altar!" is a transitional episode in the Skypiea Arc that sets up the Ordeal challenges and splits the Straw Hat crew into two groups exploring Upper Yard.

## Research Process

### Sources Consulted
- One Piece Wiki (Fandom) - primary source for episode details
- Manga Chapter 245 wiki page for cross-reference
- Multiple anime databases (IMDb, Simkl, Apple TV)
- Fan discussion forums (ResetEra)

### Key Findings

1. **Manga Adaptation**: This episode primarily adapts Chapter 245 "Adventure on God's Island" from Volume 26.

2. **Anime-Original Change**: The episode features a notable difference from the manga - Luffy defeats the Sky Lamprey with Gomu Gomu no Pistol in the anime, while Sanji defeats it in the manga. This is likely for pacing or to give Luffy more action in the episode.

3. **Arc Position**: Episode 159 is positioned in the middle section of the Skypiea Arc (episodes 153-195), specifically at the point where the crew is about to face the four priest trials.

4. **Thematic Importance**: Robin's examination of 1,000-year-old ruins foreshadows the Poneglyph discovery later in the arc and connects to the series' larger Void Century mystery.
