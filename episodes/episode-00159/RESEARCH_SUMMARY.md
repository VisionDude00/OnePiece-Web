## Episode Synopsis

The Straw Hat crew splits into two groups to explore Upper Yard. Luffy, Sanji, and Usopp navigate the dangerous Milky Road on the Karasumaru, encountering deadly traps including blade mechanisms, wooden spears, razor boomerangs, and a Sky Lamprey before arriving at the mysterious Ordeal Gates. Meanwhile, Robin, Zoro, and Nami explore ancient ruins (dated at 1,000 years old), while Chopper is left alone to guard the Going Merry.
