# Research Notes for Episode 74

## Episode Overview
Episode 74 "The Devilish Candle! Tears of Regret and Tears of Anger!" is one of the most dramatic episodes in the Little Garden arc, featuring iconic character moments and intense drama.

## Key Observations

### Zoro's Character Moment
The scene where Zoro prepares to cut off his own legs is frequently cited as one of the most impactful early moments showcasing his extreme dedication to his warrior code. This moment:
- Demonstrates Zoro's absolute refusal to accept helpless death
- Earns respect from Brogy, establishing cross-cultural warrior bonds
- Leaves physical scars (ankle scars) that Oda sometimes forgets to draw in later episodes
- Becomes a reference point for fan theories about Zoro's eventual fate

### Mr. 3's Torture Device
The Tokudai Candle Service Set represents one of the most creative and disturbing uses of a Devil Fruit power in the early series:
- Shows Mr. 3's twisted artistic vision
- Demonstrates the versatility of the Doru Doru no Mi
- Creates genuine tension and stakes for the heroes
- The wax-as-hard-as-steel concept is established clearly

### Fan Reception
The Little Garden arc has mixed reception:
- Some viewers find it slow/filler-like
- Others praise it as excellent character writing with great moments
- The Zoro leg-cutting scene is universally recognized as powerful
- Arc praised for its themes of honor, friendship, and determination

### Cultural Impact
- The wax torture scene influenced how fans view Mr. 3's threat level
- Zoro's warrior spirit moment parallels samurai honor codes
- Connection to Viking/Elbaf warrior culture through the giants' perspective
