## Episode Overview
Episode 555 "Deadly Attacks One After Another! Zoro and Sanji Join the Battle!" is a pivotal episode in the Fish-Man Island arc that showcases the Straw Hats' post-timeskip power levels, particularly focusing on Zoro and Sanji's new abilities.

## Key Observations

### 1. Power Scaling Demonstration
This episode serves as a clear demonstration of how much stronger the Straw Hats have become after the two-year timeskip. The ease with which Zoro and Sanji handle large numbers of enemies who would have been challenging before the timeskip is intentional - it shows they're now ready for the New World.

### 2. Technique Debuts
- **Kokujo O Tatsumaki (Zoro)**: Enhanced version of Tatsu Maki with Buddhist hell theming
- **Sky Walk (Sanji)**: Independently developed air-walking ability
- **Poêle à Frire: Spectre (Sanji)**: Enhanced aerial Diable Jambe technique

### 3. Flashback Integration
The episode effectively uses flashbacks to Zoro's training with Mihawk and Sanji's training on Momoiro Island to provide context for their new abilities. Notably, Sanji's flashback is longer than in the manga and includes Caroline.

### 4. Thematic Elements
The episode continues the Fish-Man Island arc's exploration of inherited hatred through Zeo's speech rallying the New Fish-Man Pirates. This reinforces the arc's central theme about racism and prejudice.

### 5. Character Focus
While all Straw Hats are present, this episode specifically spotlights:
- **Zoro**: Showcases his enhanced swordsmanship and powerful new techniques
- **Sanji**: Demonstrates aerial combat capabilities and enhanced leg strength
- **Zeo**: Reveals his role as strategist/morale officer for the enemy

2. Toonami broadcast was exactly 10 years later (June 5, 2022)
3. "Kokujo" references Kalasutra Buddhist hell where sinners are cut by black iron ropes
4. Jean Noueilles is also known as "Captain Porcupinefish"
5. This is the first major battle where all Straw Hats demonstrate post-timeskip abilities

## Episode Significance in Overall Story

Episode 555 represents a turning point where the narrative shifts from "will the Straw Hats survive?" to "how will they overcome more challenging threats?" It's intentionally positioned to show that the crew has graduated from Paradise-level threats and is ready for the New World, while also setting up the subsequent arcs where they face genuinely challenging opponents like Doflamingo, Big Mom, and Kaido.

The episode also serves the thematic purpose of contrasting earned strength (Straw Hats' training) with hollow hatred (Hody's motivation), reinforcing the arc's message about the emptiness of inherited prejudice.
