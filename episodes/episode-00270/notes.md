### Episode Summary
This episode serves as a pivotal moment in the Enies Lobby arc, featuring two major storylines:

1. **Luffy vs. Blueno** - Luffy demonstrates significant growth by breaking through CP9's Rokushiki techniques (Tekkai and Soru), setting up his upcoming Gear Second reveal.

2. **Oimo and Kashii's Backstory** - A powerful flashback reveals the World Government's 50-year deception of the two giants, creating a major turning point where enemies become allies.

### Key Themes
- **Truth vs. Deception:** The World Government's manipulation of Oimo and Kashii
- **Growth and Determination:** Luffy's increased strength and resolve
- **Loyalty and Sacrifice:** Both the King Bulls and the giants demonstrate extreme loyalty
- **Defying Authority:** Luffy's assault on the World Government's judicial island

### Notable Connections
- **Little Garden Arc Callback:** This episode directly references episodes 70-77 where the Straw Hats met Dorry and Brogy, showing One Piece's long-term narrative planning (approximately 200 episodes earlier).
- **Gear Second Setup:** While not revealed in this episode, Luffy's breaking of Blueno's defenses sets up the Gear Second debut in episodes 271-272.
- **CP9 Introduction:** Blueno first appeared in episode 238, making this his first major battle.

### Character Insights

**Luffy:**
- Shows ability to break through Tekkai (a first for the series at this point)
- Can predict and counter Soru movements
- Declares he doesn't care about the World Government's authority
- Clear growth from Water 7 arc encounters

**Blueno:**
- CP9 agent with Doriki rating of 820
- Uses Doa Doa no Mi (Door-Door Fruit)
- Master of Rokushiki techniques (Tekkai, Soru, and others)
- Realizes Luffy has become significantly stronger

**Oimo & Kashii:**
- Members of Giant Warrior Pirates under Dorry and Brogy
- Deceived by World Government for 50 years
- Believed they were serving to free their captains
- Truth revealed by Sogeking/Usopp
