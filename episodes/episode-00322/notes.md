### Final Notes
This episode represents a major milestone in One Piece - the completion of the core Straw Hat crew before the timeskip. Franky's joining is emotionally resonant because it represents:
1. The fulfillment of his dream to sail on his ship
2. Honoring Tom's legacy
3. Overcoming guilt and choosing to move forward
4. The power of found family to support individual growth

The episode succeeds in making Franky's decision feel earned through careful emotional setup across the entire Water 7 Saga.
