## Episode Overview
**Title:** "Luffy vs. Vivi! A Tearful Vow for Comrades"
**Japanese:** ルフィVSビビ！仲間に賭ける涙の誓い
**Air Date:** March 10, 2002

## Key Findings

### Major Events
1. **Crocodile's Identity Reveal** - This episode features one of the most significant revelations of the Arabasta Arc: Crocodile revealing himself as Mr. 0 to the Baroque Works officer agents.

2. **Nakama Speech** - The emotional confrontation between Luffy and Vivi is considered one of the defining moments in One Piece regarding the theme of friendship and what it means to be "nakama" (comrades).

3. **Operation Utopia** - Full revelation of Crocodile's master plan to overthrow Arabasta.

### Character Debuts
- **Toto** - Elderly resident of Yuba who serves as a symbol of unwavering loyalty to King Cobra and Arabasta.

### Location Debuts
- **Yuba** - Devastated oasis town
- **Rain Dinners** - Crocodile's casino and Baroque Works headquarters

### Thematic Significance
This episode is pivotal in establishing the series' core theme of trust and shared burden among friends. Luffy's insistence that Vivi trust her friends and accept their help represents a major turning point in Vivi's character development and her relationship with the Straw Hat crew.

- Episode adapts pages from two manga chapters (165: p.4-18, 166: p.2-19)
- Director: Ken Koyama
- Screenplay: Junki Takegami
