# Research Notes for Episode 493

## Episode Title
**Japanese:** ルフィとエース 兄弟の出会いの物語!
**Romanized:** Rufi to Ēsu: Kyōdai no Deai no Monogatari!
**English:** Luffy and Ace! The Story of How the Brothers Met!

## Research Date
2025-12-15

## Summary
Episode 493 begins the extended flashback arc showing Luffy's childhood with Ace. This episode specifically covers their first meeting when Garp brings 7-year-old Luffy to live with Curly Dadan on Mt. Colubo, where 10-year-old Ace is already being raised. The episode shows Luffy's persistent three-month pursuit of Ace's friendship, culminating in his discovery of Gray Terminal.

- **No ending theme** - Toei stopped airing ending themes from Episode 279 to Episode 1070

- Episode Director: Aya Komaki (小牧文)
- Screenwriter: Jin Tanaka (田中仁)
- Art Director: Ryūji Yoshiike (吉池隆司)

## Important Context

### Arc Significance
This episode marks the beginning of a crucial flashback arc that:
1. Provides emotional context for Ace's death in the Marineford War (Episodes 459-489)
2. Introduces Sabo (though he doesn't appear until Episode 494)
3. Explores themes of brotherhood, social inequality, and determination
4. Shows character development for both young Luffy and Ace

### Emotional Timing
The flashback begins approximately 9 months after Ace's death in Episode 483, allowing viewers time to process the emotional impact before revisiting happier memories.

### Chronology
- Episode is set **10 years before the main story** begins
- Luffy is **7 years old**
- Ace is **10 years old**
- Takes place on **Dawn Island** (Luffy's home island)
