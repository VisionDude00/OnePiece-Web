## Locations

### Kuraigana Island (Gloom Island)
- Former Shikkearu Kingdom destroyed by war 9 years prior
- Giant cross-shaped gravestone is key landmark
- Home to weapon-wielding Humandrills
- Mihawk's residence (castle over lake)
- Perpetual fog and gloomy atmosphere
- Paradise region of Grand Line

### Namakura Island
- Name means "lazy/cowardly" in Japanese
- Home to Harahettania ("Land of Poverty" - "hara hetta" = "I'm hungry")
- Feudal Japanese-style architecture in disrepair
- Barren landscape with dry soil
- Satan-worshiping cult led by Pekkori
- Paradise region of Grand Line
