## Episode Description

Law infiltrates the SAD production facility while Doflamingo is revealed as the mastermind orchestrating events from Dressrosa. After ordering Vergo to eliminate Law, Doflamingo dispatches reinforcements to Punk Hazard. Meanwhile, the Straw Hats defeat a dragon and learn that Momonosuke transformed after entering a forbidden room.

## Key Highlights

### Major Plot Points
1. **Law Reaches SAD Room** - Trafalgar Law successfully infiltrates the SAD production facility, achieving his primary objective
2. **Doflamingo Revealed as Mastermind** - First major scene establishing Doflamingo's role as puppet master behind Punk Hazard events
3. **Vergo's True Allegiance Confirmed** - Marine Vice Admiral Vergo revealed as Doflamingo's undercover agent
4. **Law's Execution Ordered** - Doflamingo orders Vergo to kill Law "with absolutely no mercy"
5. **Multiple Character Debuts** - Six Donquixote Pirates officers debut simultaneously
6. **Momonosuke's Secret Revealed** - The small dragon is revealed to be Momonosuke who ate an artificial Devil Fruit
7. **Yuki Yuki no Mi Named** - Monet officially reveals her Devil Fruit as the Snow-Snow Fruit

### Devil Fruits Featured
