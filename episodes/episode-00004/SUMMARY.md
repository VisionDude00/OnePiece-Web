## Episode Overview

Episode 4 is one of the most emotionally significant episodes in One Piece, revealing the origin story of Luffy's iconic straw hat and his relationship with Shanks. The episode splits its time between a 12-year flashback to Luffy's childhood in Foosha Village and the present-day arrival at Orange Town.

### Flashback Story (Romance Dawn)
Young Luffy (age 7) befriends the pirate Shanks and his Red Hair Pirates who are staying in Foosha Village. After accidentally eating the Gomu Gomu no Mi Devil Fruit, Luffy gains rubber powers but loses his ability to swim. When mountain bandit Higuma kidnaps Luffy and throws him into the ocean, Shanks arrives to save him but sacrifices his left arm to the Lord of the Coast, a Sea King. Before departing for the Grand Line, Shanks entrusts his treasured straw hat to Luffy, making him promise to return it when he becomes a great pirate.

### Present Day Story (Orange Town)
Luffy and Zoro arrive at Orange Town and encounter Nami for the first time, a thief who has stolen treasure from Buggy the Clown's crew.
