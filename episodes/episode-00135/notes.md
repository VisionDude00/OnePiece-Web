# Research Notes for Episode 135

## Episode Overview
Episode 135 "The Fabled Pirate Hunter! Zoro, the Wandering Swordsman!" is the final episode of the Post-Arabasta Arc and marks the conclusion of the Arabasta Saga. It is an anime-original filler episode that provides backstory for Zoro's life as a bounty hunter before joining the Straw Hat Pirates.

## Key Findings

### Episode Significance

1. **Character Development**: This episode completes the Post-Arabasta Arc's exploration of Straw Hat crew members' backstories and dreams. Each episode in the arc focused on a different crew member (Chopper, Nami, Sanji, Usopp, and finally Zoro).

2. **Johnny and Yosaku Origin**: This is the only episode that shows how Zoro first met Johnny and Yosaku, providing context for their later appearances in the Baratie and Arlong Park arcs.

3. **Canon Conflicts**: The episode creates several continuity errors with later canon material, particularly regarding Zoro's swords.

### Continuity Issues Identified

1. **Wado Ichimonji Repair**: The episode shows the Wado Ichimonji being repaired by a swordsmith, which contradicts its established status as one of the 21 Great Grade swords with exceptional durability.

2. **Missing Katanas**: Zoro is depicted without his original pair of katanas during a time when, according to later canon (revealed around Chapter 1033/Episode 1053), he should still have had them. These were childhood gifts from Shimotsuki Kouzaburou that weren't destroyed until his duel with Mihawk.

3. **Filler vs. Novel**: Interestingly, scenes from this filler episode were later adapted into "One Piece novel ZORO" chapter 2, suggesting some elements were retroactively incorporated into semi-canon material.

### Production Details

- **Air Date**: December 1, 2002 (Japan)
- **TV Rating**: 11.8% in Japan, ranking 8th for its broadcast period
- **4Kids Dub**: This is the only Post-Arabasta Arc episode included in the 4Kids dub (as Episode 99)
