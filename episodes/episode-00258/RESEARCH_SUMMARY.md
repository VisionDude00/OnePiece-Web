## Research Highlights

### Cultural Impact
- Sogeking theme became one of the most beloved songs in One Piece
- Animation parodies classic Toei tokusatsu superhero shows
- Song returned in Episode 1015 (Wano Arc), demonstrating lasting popularity
- Multiple fan covers and reactions available online

### Character Development
- Showcases Usopp's pride and loyalty in conflict
- Demonstrates Sanji's emotional intelligence
- Highlights unspoken bonds between crew members
- Shows Usopp's continued care through the Perfect Clima-Tact upgrade

### Production Quality
- 11.4% TV viewership rating in Japan (5th rank)
- Extended Sogeking sequence with anime-original elaborate animation
- Strong voice performances, especially Kappei Yamaguchi as Sogeking
