## Historical Significance
This episode was censored in its initial Japanese broadcast due to the Akihabara massacre that occurred just two weeks before the air date (June 8, 2008). Stabbing scenes were altered to kicks in the TV version, though the original scenes were restored in the DVD release and international versions.
