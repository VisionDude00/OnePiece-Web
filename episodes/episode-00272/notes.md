- Despite being an older episode, it continues to generate reaction content and analysis in 2024-2025

- Casualty count of over 2,000 World Government forces emphasizes the unprecedented scale of the Enies Lobby assault
- The episode ends on a cliffhanger with Gear 2's activation, with the full demonstration occurring in Episode 273
