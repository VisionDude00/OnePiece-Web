## Notable Findings

### 1. Anime-Original Change
**IMPORTANT:** Episode 212 contains a significant anime-original plot change. In the manga (Chapter 313), Luffy chooses Chopper after winning the Groggy Ring. The anime changes this to have Luffy choose Shelly instead, extending Chopper's captivity to build dramatic tension for the final round.

This is documented as a MAJOR_PLOT_CHANGE in mangaDifferences.

### 2. First Appearances
- **Mutouryuu: Tatsu Maki** (Zoro's No Sword Style) - First animated appearance
- **Troisième Hachis** (Sanji technique)
- **Bouquetière Shot** (Sanji technique)
- **Armée de l'Air Power Shoot** (Zoro/Sanji combination)

### 3. Historic Moment
The Groggy Monsters (Hamburg, Pickles, Big Pan) had NEVER lost a Davy Back Fight match before this episode. This is their first-ever defeat.

### 4. Arc Re-evaluation
Recent manga revelations about Rocks D. Xebec have caused the One Piece community to re-evaluate the Long Ring Long Land arc. What was once dismissed as "filler" or "the worst arc" is now recognized as important worldbuilding and potential foreshadowing.
