## Research Summary

This episode marks a pivotal moment in the series with the reveal of Garp as Luffy's grandfather. The research was comprehensive, drawing from multiple sources including the One Piece Wiki, contemporary blog posts from 2007, and various fan analyses.

1. **Garp's Grandfather Reveal**: The central event of the episode where Luffy casually calls Garp "Gramps," shocking everyone
2. **Fist of Love Debut**: Garp demonstrates his ability to hurt Luffy despite his rubber body
3. **Treasure Tree Adam Announcement**: Franky reveals his plans to build the new ship using legendary wood
4. **Koby and Helmeppo Reunion**: The two Marines reunite with Luffy, showing their growth

### Character Debuts

## Research Challenges & Questions

### 1. Episode Script Access
**Challenge**: Could not locate a full, detailed episode script or transcript for Episode 313.

**Resolution**: Used multiple wiki sources, blog recaps, and cross-referenced with Chapter 431 of the manga to piece together events and dialogue.

**Impact**: Timestamps are approximate rather than exact. Dialogue quotes are based on commonly referenced versions rather than verified transcripts.

### 2. Exact Timestamps
**Challenge**: Without access to the actual episode video or a timestamped script, precise timestamps were difficult to determine.

**Resolution**: Estimated timestamps based on typical episode structure:
- Opening: ~0:00-2:00
- First Act: ~3:00-8:00
- Second Act: ~8:00-13:00
- Third Act: ~13:00-18:00
- Climax: ~18:00-22:00

**Impact**: All timestamps in events are estimates within 30-60 second accuracy.

### 3. Voice Actor Information
**Challenge**: Could not verify all minor character voice actors (Michael and Hoichael).

**Resolution**: Left voice actor fields empty for minor characters where information was unavailable.

### 4. Garp's First Appearance Clarification
**Important Note**: Garp actually first appeared in Episode 68 (and Chapter 92 in cover story), NOT Episode 313. Episode 313 is when his **relationship to Luffy** is revealed. This distinction is important for accuracy.

**Resolution**: Noted Garp's actual debut episode in character data while documenting Episode 313 as where the family relationship is revealed.

### 5. "Fist of Love" - Haki or Love?
**Research Finding**: Eiichiro Oda stated in SBS Volume 62 that Garp's "Fist of Love" works through "love," not Haki. However, this is widely believed by fans to be a comedic deflection, with most theorizing Garp uses Armament Haki.

**Resolution**: Documented both Oda's official explanation and the popular fan theory in the appropriate files.

### 6. Opening/Ending Songs
**Finding**: Episode 313 uses "Crazy Rainbow" by Tackey & Tsubasa as the opening (episodes 284-325).

**Important Note**: There is NO ending song for Episode 313. Toei Animation stopped airing ending themes from Episode 279 onwards, extending opening themes to 150 seconds instead.

### 7. External Media Limitations
**Challenge**: YouTube search was unavailable during research, limiting discovery of reaction videos and analysis content.

**Resolution**: Found one Bilibili reaction compilation and several articles/blog posts. Documented what was available through web search.

**Suggestion**: Future researchers should search YouTube directly for:
- "One Piece Episode 313 reaction"
- "Garp grandfather reveal reaction"
- "One Piece episode 313 review"
