# Research Notes for Episode 298

## Episode Overview
Episode 298 "Fiery Kicks! Sanji's Full Course of Foot Techniques!" is a pivotal episode in the Enies Lobby Arc, featuring Sanji's battle with CP9's Jabra and the debut of the iconic Diable Jambe technique.

## Key Research Findings

### Diable Jambe Debut
- This is the first appearance of Diable Jambe, one of Sanji's most iconic techniques
- The technique debuted in both manga Chapter 415 "Heat Up" (June 19, 2006) and anime Episode 298 (February 25, 2007)
- Oda's SBS explanation: Sanji generates flames through friction by spinning at high speed; he doesn't burn because "his heart burns hotter than his flames"
- Two specific Diable Jambe techniques shown:
  1. Diable Jambe: Premier Hachis (rapid dual-leg kicks)
  2. Diable Jambe: Flambage Shot (finishing aerial side kick)

### Battle Context
- Jabra is CP9's third-strongest member with a Doriki of 2180
- Jabra's devil fruit: Inu Inu no Mi, Model: Wolf
- Jabra uses Tekkai Kenpo, a unique style allowing movement during Tekkai
- Jabra attempts deception claiming Robin is his sister (shown with cartoon-style animation)
- Despite landing his Gekko Jusshigan attack, Jabra is defeated

### Production Details
- Air date: February 25, 2007 (Japan), October 15, 2013 (US)
- Opening: "Crazy Rainbow" by Tackey & Tsubasa (episodes 284-325)
- Ending: None (One Piece stopped using ending songs after episode 279)
- Director: Hiroyuki Kakudō
- Screenplay: Naoki Koga
- IMDB Rating: 8.6/10

### Animation Notes
- Cartoon-style animation used during Jabra's deceptive story
- Eye catches featured Sanji and Usopp in swapped positions
- Extended fight choreography compared to manga source material
