Key moments include Zoro getting lost on a straight road (comedic character moment), Sanji abandoning the race to save Porche (chivalry weakness), and Nami/Usopp nearly winning through strategy. The Straw Hats ultimately lose, and Chopper is taken by the Foxy Pirates again.

## Additional Notes

### Cultural Context
"Daruma-san ga Koronda" is a traditional Japanese children's game similar to "Red Light, Green Light" or "Grandmother's Footsteps." The name literally means "the Daruma doll fell down." Understanding this cultural reference adds depth to the episode's themes.

### Arc Controversy
The Long Ring Long Land arc is consistently ranked among the least popular One Piece arcs. The anime's decision to add three additional filler games (including this episode's Red Light Green Light game) significantly contributed to this negative reception. Many fans recommend skipping episodes 213-216 when watching One Piece.

### Vindication Through Time
Interestingly, what was once dismissed as the series' worst arc has recently gained new appreciation. The revelation that Rocks D. Xebec—one of the series' most important historical figures—regularly participated in Davy Back Fights has caused fans to reconsider the arc's purpose. This is a fascinating case study in how foreshadowing can take 20+ years to pay off.

### Research Limitations
This research was conducted without direct access to the episode video or official scripts. All information is synthesized from secondary sources. For academic or professional purposes, primary source verification is recommended.
