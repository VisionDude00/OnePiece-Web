## Notable Discoveries

### 1. Cultural Connection
The episode aired on May 5, 2013, which is Children's Day (Kodomo no Hi) in Japan. This is thematically significant given the episode's subplot about saving children from Caesar's experiments.

### 2. Devil Fruit Rarity
The Sara Sara no Mi, Model: Axolotl is one of the rare examples of a Devil Fruit being fed to an inanimate object (Smiley, the gas slime). This technique was pioneered by Dr. Vegapunk.

### 3. Robin's Foreshadowing
Robin's warning about pirate alliances ending in betrayal generated significant fan speculation for years, even though Law never betrayed the alliance. This demonstrates the show's success in creating dramatic tension.

### 4. Long-Term Planning
The fact that Law's plan had only a 30% success rate, yet both crews committed to it, showcases the high-risk, high-reward nature of New World navigation.
