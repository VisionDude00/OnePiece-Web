### Key Findings

#### Venom Demon Technique
- Japanese: ベノムデーモン: 地獄の審判 (Benomu Dēmon: Jigoku no Shinpan)
- Translation: "Venom Demon: Hell's Judgment"
- Described as a "forbidden technique" (禁じ手 - kinjite)
- Crimson-red poison that can destroy even non-organic materials
- Powerful enough to threaten the entire structure of Impel Down

#### Champion Techniques
- Champion Gatling and Champion Stamp are anime-exclusive combination techniques
- Not present in the original manga
- Showcase tactical cooperation between Luffy and Galdino (Mr. 3)

#### Voice Actor Note
- Katsuhisa Hōki voices Jinbe in this episode, having taken over from Daisuke Gōri starting at episode 440
- Daisuke Gōri passed away in January 2010, just months before this episode aired

#### Historical Significance
- First and only mass breakout in Impel Down's history
- Over 200 prisoners escaped
- Led to Magellan's demotion from Chief Warden to Vice Warden
- TV Rating: 12.5% (Rank #2 in timeslot)
