# Research Notes for Episode 407

## Episode Overview
Episode 407 "Feudal Era Side Story - Defeat Thriller Company's Trap" is the second part of a two-episode special featuring Boss Luffy in a feudal Japanese setting (Grand Jipangu). This is anime-original filler content not based on any manga chapters.

## Key Research Findings

### Episode Classification
- **Filler Episode**: 100% anime-original content
- **Part of Boss Luffy Historical Special**: A recurring special arc featuring alternate universe versions of One Piece characters
- **Setting**: Grand Jipangu, an alternate universe version of Edo period Japan
- **Aired**: June 28, 2009 (between Sabaody Archipelago arc episodes)

### Production Notes
- **Final Episode with "Share the World" Opening**: This was the last episode to use TVXQ's "Share the World" (episodes 395-425) before transitioning to "Kaze wo Sagashite" starting with episode 408
- **No Ending Theme**: Like all episodes from 279-1070, this episode had no ending theme
- **Credits**:
  - Screenplay: Jin Tanaka
  - Animation Director: Masahiro Shimanuki
  - Art Director: Miyuki Sato
  - Direction: Naoyuki Itō (from initial research)

### Reception
- **IMDb Rating**: 5.6/10 (based on 837 ratings)
- **Lower Rating Explained**: As a filler episode disconnected from the main story, it received a lower rating than canon episodes
- **Fan Reception**: Generally viewed as lighthearted fun, providing relief between intense canon arcs

### Plot Structure
The episode follows a clear three-act structure:
1. **Setup (00:00-10:00)**: Robin reveals Brook's past, crew decides to help, Brook is captured
2. **Conflict (10:00-20:00)**: Mikoshi race with various complications and sabotage attempts
3. **Resolution (20:00-23:00)**: Victory, prize reveal, comedic ending

### Character Notes

#### Mao (Debut)
- First appearance in this episode
- Based on actress Mao Kobayashi
- Waitress at Pinwheel Eatery
- Plays key role in getting cola and providing comedic ending
- No-nonsense personality demonstrated by punching Brook

- Has recurring memory loss issue (established in episode 406)
