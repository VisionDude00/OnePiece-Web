## Notable Details

### Fist of Love Controversy
There's an interesting debate about Garp's "Fist of Love" technique. The episode wiki states it shows "early usage of Armament Haki," but Oda (the creator) has explicitly stated in SBS that it works because of "love" rather than Haki. I documented both perspectives in the trivia and marked the Haki theory as "DEBUNKED" in fan theories.

### Opening/Ending Songs
Episode 314 uses "Crazy Rainbow" by Tackey & Tsubasa (episodes 284-325). There is no ending song because the anime stopped using ending themes after Episode 279, instead opting for longer openings.
