# Episode 505 Research Notes

## Research Completed: December 16, 2025

### Episode Overview
Episode 505 "I Want to See Them! Luffy's Tearful Scream" is one of the most emotionally powerful episodes in One Piece. It marks a crucial turning point in Luffy's character development as he processes Ace's death with Jinbe's help.

This episode explores several deep themes:
- **Grief and Loss** - Different characters process Ace's death differently

- **Duty vs. Family** - Garp's impossible position

### 8. Jinbe's Speech - The Heart of the Episode
The speech is consistently ranked as one of the top moments in all of One Piece:

**Quote:** "Do not simply count what you have lost. What's gone is gone! But ask yourself this, What remains?"

**Significance:**
- Pulls Luffy from the brink of complete despair
- Represents a fundamental shift in Luffy's character philosophy
- Foreshadows Jinbe's eventual joining of the Straw Hat crew
- One of the most emotionally resonant moments in the entire series

## Significance of Episode 505

This episode represents one of the most crucial emotional moments in One Piece:

1. **Character Development:** Luffy's lowest point and beginning of recovery
2. **Series Themes:** Reinforces core theme of nakama bonds
3. **Story Progression:** Sets up the timeskip and Luffy's training
4. **Fan Reception:** Consistently ranked as top emotional moment
5. **Jinbe's Role:** Establishes why he belongs in the Straw Hat crew

The "What remains?" speech is not just a great moment - it's a defining philosophy that shapes Luffy's approach for the rest of the series.
