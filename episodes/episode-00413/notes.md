## Key Observations

### Significance of Episode 413
This is arguably one of the most important episodes in the pre-timeskip era for several reasons:

1. **First Confirmed Conqueror's Haki:** While Luffy unconsciously used it in Episode 1, this is the first time it's officially recognized and named as Supreme King Haki.

2. **Introduction to Haki System:** The episode demonstrates all three types of Haki in action:
   - Observation Haki (Sandersonia's Snake Dance)
   - Armament Haki (both sisters attacking Luffy)
   - Conqueror's Haki (Luffy's burst)

3. **Power Gap Revelation:** Luffy learns his Devil Fruit immunity is not absolute, setting up his need for Haki training.

4. **Character Development:** The trigger for Luffy's Haki (protecting Marguerite) reinforces his core character trait of protecting friends above all else.

5. **Relationship Building:** Hancock witnessing Luffy's Supreme King Haki begins her respect and eventual romantic feelings for him.

### Manga Milestone
Chapter 519 (adapted in this episode) was notable for reaching the same chapter count as the original Dragon Ball manga (519 chapters), marked with special gray shading on the manga cover. This milestone wasn't reflected in the anime but is worth noting for historical context.
