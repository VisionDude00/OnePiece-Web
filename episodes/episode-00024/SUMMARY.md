## Synopsis
Zoro challenges the legendary Dracule Mihawk, one of the Seven Warlords of the Sea and the world's greatest swordsman, to a duel. Despite fighting with all his strength, Zoro is completely overwhelmed and severely wounded. In defeat, Zoro makes a solemn vow to Luffy and himself that he will never lose again until he becomes the world's greatest swordsman.

## Why This Episode Matters
This is widely considered one of the most important episodes in One Piece history. It:
- Establishes Zoro's character arc for the entire series
- Introduces the Shichibukai system
- Shows the power gap between East Blue and Grand Line
- Creates one of anime's most iconic character vows
- Demonstrates the importance of honor and determination over raw power
