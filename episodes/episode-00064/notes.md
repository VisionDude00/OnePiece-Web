# Research Notes for Episode 64

## Episode Information
- **Title**: "A Town that Welcomes Pirates! Setting Foot on Whisky Peak!"
- **Japanese**: 海賊歓迎の町？ウイスキーピーク上陸 (Kaizoku kangei no machi? Uisukīpīku jōriku)
- **Air Date**: April 15, 2001 (Japan), July 30, 2005 (US - 4Kids)
- **Arc**: Whisky Peak Arc (First episode)
- **Saga**: Arabasta Saga

## Key Research Findings

### 1. Production Notes
- This was the first episode broadcast on Sunday evenings after a timeslot change
- Episodes 64 and 65 aired together as a television special
- First episode to use "Watashi ga Iru Yo" as the ending theme
- Covers manga chapters 106 (pages 2-19) and 107 (pages 3-13)
- 4Kids combined this with episodes 61 and 62 into their episode 44

### 2. Character Debuts
**New Characters:**
- **Igaram (Mr. 8)**: Mayor of Whisky Peak, captain of Arabasta's royal guard undercover as a Baroque Works agent
- **Miss Monday**: Baroque Works frontier agent, partner to Mr. 8, disguised as a nun

**Returning Characters:**
- Vivi (Miss Wednesday) and Mr. 9 return from previous episodes

### 3. Location Debuts
- **Cactus Island**: First Grand Line island visited by the Straw Hats
- **Whisky Peak**: Town entirely inhabited by Baroque Works bounty hunters
- **Cactus Island Graveyard**: Distinctive graveyard with cactus-shaped rock formations

### 4. Major Events
1. Straw Hats arrive at first Grand Line island
2. Nami demonstrates essential navigation skills
3. Welcome feast that's actually a trap
4. Baroque Works agents revealed
5. Zoro remains vigilant while others sleep
6. Setup for major battle in Episode 65

### 5. Thematic Elements
- **Deception vs. Trust**: The friendly welcome masks deadly intentions
- **Vigilance**: Zoro's decision to stay alert saves the crew
- **Grand Line Dangers**: Not just weather, but organized crime networks

### 6. Research Challenges
- Limited access to specific YouTube analyses and Reddit discussions due to web search limitations
- No specific episode script found, relied on wiki summaries and episode information
- Fan theories specifically about Episode 64 were not readily available

### 7. Notable Details
- Zoro's philosophy: "A true swordsman never allows alcohol to take control of himself"
- Miss Monday secretly drank barley tea instead of alcohol in the drinking contest
- The town planned to capture the crew alive for full bounty value
- Luffy's 30,000,000 berry bounty is specifically mentioned

### 8. Cross-References
- **Setup episodes**: 61, 62, 63 (traveling on Grand Line with Vivi and Mr. 9)
- **Payoff episodes**: 65, 66, 67 (continuation of Whisky Peak arc)
- **Callback to**: Episode 45 (Luffy's bounty from Arlong Park arc)

### 9. Animation & Production Quality
- Rated as STANDARD animation quality
- Animation Director: Yoko Ikeda
- Storyboard: Kenji Yokoyama
- Screenplay: Michiru Shimada
- Art Direction: Miyuki Sato
- Key Animator: Kazuo Takigawa

### 10. Cultural Context
- First major introduction of Baroque Works organization
- Establishes the Grand Line as having both natural and human threats
- Shows the consequences of having a bounty in the pirate world
