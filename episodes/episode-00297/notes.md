# Episode 297 Research Notes

## Research Date
November 18, 2025

## Episode Overview
Episode 297 "Hunter Sanji Makes an Entrance? Elegy for a Lying Wolf!" is a pivotal episode in the Enies Lobby arc that sets up Sanji's major battle against CP9's Jabra.

## Key Findings

### Fairy Tale Theme
This episode has a strong Little Red Riding Hood motif:
- Sanji introduces himself as "The Hunter"
- Jabra is in his wolf Zoan form
- Robin is shown in imagery wearing a red-hooded cloak
- This creates a fairy tale framework for their battle

The anime appears to have enhanced these visual references compared to the manga.

### Ending Theme Discontinuation
Episode 297 is notable for being part of the period when One Piece had no ending themes. Ending themes were discontinued after Episode 278 (which used "Adventure World" by Delicatessen) and didn't return until Episode 1071 in 2023 - representing a 17-year gap.

### Jabra's Character
Jabra is established as a particularly deceptive fighter who uses psychological warfare:
1. He tricks Sogeking by pretending to be a pacifist
2. He attempts to trick Sanji with the fake "Robin is my sister" story
3. His wolf Devil Fruit connects to folklore about deceptive wolves

### Episode Structure
The episode effectively balances multiple plot threads:
- Sanji vs Jabra (primary focus)
- Zoro vs Kaku (secondary)
- Luffy vs Rob Lucci (tertiary)
- Franky rescuing Chopper and heading to Gates of Justice

While Jabra's Inu Inu no Mi, Model: Wolf is featured, it was previously revealed in Episode 286. No new abilities are demonstrated in this specific episode.

### Diable Jambe Note
While this episode begins the Sanji vs Jabra fight, Diable Jambe (Sanji's fire-based technique) debuts in the NEXT episode (298), not this one. This episode is purely setup for that reveal.

## Cultural References

The Little Red Riding Hood theme is the primary cultural reference in this episode. The story elements align well:
- Big Bad Wolf = Jabra (literally a wolf)
- Hunter = Sanji (saves the day)
- Red Riding Hood = Robin (shown in red hood imagery)
- Deception = Central to both the fairy tale and Jabra's tactics
