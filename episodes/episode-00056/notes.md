**Arc:** Warship Island Arc (Episodes 54-61)
**Type:** Filler (Non-canon)

## Research Summary

This episode is part of the first full filler arc in One Piece, created to allow the manga to get ahead of the anime production. The episode focuses on the Straw Hats' attempt to escape from Warship Island with Apis and the ancient dragon Ryu while being pursued by Marines and the mercenary Eric.

## Key Characters Featured
- **Eric "The Whirlwind"** - Main antagonist, Kama Kama no Mi user
- **Apis** - Young girl with Hiso Hiso no Mi ability
- **Straw Hat Pirates** - All five members (Luffy, Zoro, Nami, Usopp, Sanji)
- **Ryu** - The ancient Sennenryu dragon
- **Nelson Royal** - Marine Commodore (mentioned)
