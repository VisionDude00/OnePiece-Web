## Episode Synopsis
The Straw Hats and their allies aboard the Rocketman sea train race toward Enies Lobby to rescue Robin. In a spectacular sequence, Yokozuna the giant frog bends the outer gates to create a ramp, launching the train into the air. The flying Rocketman soars over the defenses and crashes into Oimo, knocking the giant guard unconscious. Meanwhile, Robin and Franky are brought before Spandam, the leader of CP9, at the Tower of Justice. The episode introduces Baskerville, a unique three-person character serving as Chief Justice, and the Watchdog Unit of the Law, Enies Lobby's elite guards.
