## Notable Discoveries

### Perfect Clima-Tact Debut
This episode is significant for being Nami's first combat use of the Perfect Clima-Tact. While Usopp created it in Episode 257, this is where we see its devastating (and uncontrollable) power in action.

### Aokiji's Role
Major revelation that Admiral Aokiji:
- Informed Spandam of Robin's location in Water 7
- Gave Spandam the Golden Den Den Mushi (Buster Call authority)
- Directly enabled the current crisis

This is a critical plot point that connects back to Episodes 227-229 (Long Ring Long Land Arc) where Aokiji first encountered the Straw Hats.

### Paulie's Message
"You're fired!" - Simple but powerful message representing Galley-La's formal rejection of CP9 members who infiltrated their company. Callbacks to the Water 7 Arc revelations in Episodes 238-240.

### Television Special
Episodes 268 and 269 originally aired together as a television special on June 25, 2006, explaining the connected narrative flow.
