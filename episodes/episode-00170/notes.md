## Cultural References

### Buddhist Philosophy
The 36 Pound Cannon technique name derives from:
- 6 senses (sight, sound, smell, taste, touch, thought)
- Each can be good, bad, or neutral (3 states) = 18 combinations
- Each can be pure or tainted (2 states) = 36 total
- Represents the 36 worldly desires/earthly attachments in Buddhism

This shows Zoro's techniques often incorporate philosophical and cultural depth.
