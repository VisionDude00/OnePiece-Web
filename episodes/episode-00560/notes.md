## Notable Findings

### Anime-Original Content
**Nuru** is completely anime-original:
- Does not appear in manga Chapter 639
- Created to extend Zoro's screen time
- Dies in the same episode he debuts
- Serves to show Hyouzou's loss of control

### Manga Differences
1. Nuru subplot entirely anime-original
2. Hody's pursuit of Shirahoshi extended in anime
3. Hyouzou's transformation shown more prominently in anime

### Production Details
- **No Ending Theme:** Episodes 279-1070 (17 years) had no ending themes to allow longer openings
- **Opening:** "We Go!" by Hiroshi Kitadani (described by artist as "response to We Are!")
- **TV Rating:** 8.7% in Japan (Rank 5 for time slot)
- **IMDb Rating:** 7.3/10 (below series average, likely due to arc pacing issues)

### Thematic Elements
This episode is part of the Fish-Man Island arc's exploration of:
- **Racism and inherited prejudice** (Hody never personally experienced discrimination)
- **Consequences of shortcuts to power** (Energy Steroids vs Straw Hats' training)
- **Post-timeskip strength demonstration** (Luffy fighting underwater, Zoro's confidence)
- **Protection and determination** (overcoming environmental disadvantages)
