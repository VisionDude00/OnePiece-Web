## Notable Findings

### Technical Excellence
The use of classical music (Dvořák) for the climactic battle demonstrates the anime production team's commitment to elevating key moments beyond typical shonen scoring.

### Symbolic Resonance
The rain serves as a multi-layered symbol: cleansing (washing away manipulation), renewal (kingdom can heal), hope (the impossible becomes possible), and truth (confusion is cleared).

### Technique Innovation
Gomu Gomu no Storm showcases Luffy's creative problem-solving by combining inflation, rotation, and momentum - demonstrating understanding of his powers beyond simple stretching.

### Narrative Milestone
This is Luffy's first defeat of a Warlord, proving the Straw Hats can challenge the World Government's power structure - a major escalation in the series' stakes.
