## Cultural Significance

### Why This Episode Matters
- Ranked #4 in "Saddest Goodbyes in One Piece" (CBR)
- Features one of the most iconic scenes in the entire series
- The X mark has become a lasting symbol in One Piece culture
- Scene was reanimated for "WE ARE ONE" 1000th episode project
- Consistently cited as a fan-favorite emotional moment

### The X Mark Legacy
- Originally created to counter Mr. 2's abilities (Episode 127)
- Transformed into symbol of eternal friendship
- Referenced in recent manga chapters (Chapter 1130, 2024)
- Popular tattoo among One Piece fans
- Represents friendship that transcends physical presence
