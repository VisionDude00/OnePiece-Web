- Splits the narrative between Water 7 (Nami, Luffy, Chopper, Zoro) and the Sea Train (Sanji, Robin, Usopp, Franky)
- Introduces the ticking clock of Aqua Laguna
- First shows a CP6 agent, establishing Cipher Pol hierarchy
- Demonstrates Sanji's willingness to act independently for nakama

### Animation and Pacing
- Standard animation quality for Water 7 arc
- Episode balances action (Sanji vs Jerry) with tension-building (Aqua Laguna approach)
- Uses natural disaster as compelling time pressure
- Relatively self-contained battle sequence within larger arc narrative

### Character Development
- **Sanji:** Shows growth in independent action and strategic thinking
- **Nami:** Leadership role becomes more prominent; coordinates scattered crew
- **Paulie:** Reinforces heroic ally status and local knowledge expertise

### World Building
- Expands understanding of Cipher Pol organization (CP6 vs CP9 distinction)
