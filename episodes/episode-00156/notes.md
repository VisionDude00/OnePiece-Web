# Research Notes for Episode 156

## Episode Overview
Episode 156 "Already Criminals?! Skypiea's Upholder of the Law!" is a crucial episode in the Skypiea Arc that establishes the conflict between the Straw Hat Pirates and the law enforcement of Skypiea. The episode serves as a transition from the exploratory phase of the arc to the action-heavy confrontation with Enel's forces.

## Key Observations

### Episode Significance
1. **Musical Milestone**: This is the last episode to use "Free Will" as the ending theme - a notable production detail.
2. **Technique Debut**: Gomu Gomu no Hanabi makes its anime debut, expanding Luffy's combat repertoire.
3. **Character Introduction**: McKinley is introduced as a significant supporting character who will play a larger role later in the arc.
4. **Plot Escalation**: The episode escalates the conflict from a simple misunderstanding to a serious confrontation requiring the Priests' involvement.

### Thematic Elements
1. **Justice vs Authority**: The episode strongly explores themes of unjust laws and authoritarian control.
2. **Escalation**: Shows how oppressive systems can criminalize innocent behavior.
3. **Power Disparity**: Demonstrates the gap between the Straw Hats and ordinary Skypiea forces.

### World Building
1. **Crime Classification System**: Introduces Skypiea's unique legal system with different crime classes.
2. **Law Enforcement Hierarchy**: Establishes the White Berets as the lowest level, with Priests handling serious crimes.
3. **Ordeals Introduction**: Sets up the deadly trials that will be featured in upcoming episodes.
