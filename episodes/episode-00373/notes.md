# Research Notes for Episode 373

## Episode Overview
**Title:** The End of the Battle is Nigh! Pound in the Finishing Move
**Air Date:** October 5, 2008
**Research Date:** November 30, 2025

## Key Highlights

### Opening Theme Milestone
This episode marks a significant production milestone as the first to feature the "We Are!" 2008 remix by Tohoshinki (TVXQ). This was created specifically to commemorate the One Piece anime's 10th anniversary (1999-2009). The opening ran for episodes 373-394.

### Coordinated Battle Strategy
The defeat of Oars showcases what many consider one of the best team battles in shonen anime. Every single Straw Hat crew member (all 9 at this point) contributes their unique abilities:
- **Robin:** Creates footholds with Pierna Fleur
- **Brook:** Provides speed and mobility to transport Luffy
- **Nami:** Weather manipulation to dampen Oars
- **Franky & Usopp:** Engineering/freezing combo
- **Sanji:** Physical restraint with chains
- **Chopper:** Medical/tactical analysis
- **Zoro:** Spine straightening with ultimate technique
- **Luffy:** Finishing blow with Gear 3

This represents the philosophy that teamwork and strategy can overcome raw power.

### Shadows Asgard - Power Scaling
Moria's transformation is particularly notable from a power-scaling perspective:
- Nightmare Luffy absorbed 100 shadows
- Moria absorbs 1,000 shadows (10x increase)
- However, the transformation is grotesque and appears unsustainable
- Sacrifices ALL zombie forces for personal power
- Creates a massive fissure in Thriller Bark with one punch

This technique represents the "glass cannon" archetype - enormous power but significant drawbacks.

### Voice Acting Note
Both Japanese (Mayumi Tanaka) and English (Colleen Clinkenbeard) voice actresses for Luffy also voice Oars in this episode. This makes sense narratively since Oars has Luffy's shadow and personality.

### Animation Quality
Contemporary reviews noted that while the episode wasn't as "epic" as some hoped for the Oars defeat, the Shadows Asgard transformation sequence was beautifully animated and became a highlight of the episode.

### Manga Adaptation
Episode 373 adapts:
- Chapter 480: "Interception" (pages 2-19)
- Chapter 481: (pages 2-13)

The anime expanded the coordinated attack sequence with additional animation showing each crew member's contribution in more detail than the manga panels.

## Additional Observations

### Episode Pacing
This episode represents excellent pacing for the climax of a long battle arc. It:
1. Starts with the recovery/decision to fight
2. Shows the coordinated attack sequence
3. Delivers the victory moment
4. Immediately introduces the next threat

No wasted time, constant forward momentum.

### Thematic Consistency
The episode reinforces One Piece's core themes:
- Dreams and determination (Straw Hats refuse to give up)
- Teamwork over individual strength
- Strategic intelligence versus raw power
- Escalating challenges (defeating one threat reveals another)

### Character Development
While primarily an action episode, it showcases:
- **Chopper's** value beyond healing (medical analysis)
- **Brook's** integration into the crew (first major team battle)
- **Moria's** desperation (willing to sacrifice everything)
