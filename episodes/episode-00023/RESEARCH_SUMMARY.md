### Character Revelations

**Mihawk**:
- World's greatest swordsman
- Seven Warlords of the Sea member
- Destroyed Krieg's entire fleet (50 ships, 5,000 men) single-handedly
- Followed them to East Blue "to kill time"
- Hawks eyes with concentric rings give him his epithet

**Zeff**:
- Former captain of Cook Pirates
- Earned epithet "Red Leg Zeff" from kicks stained with enemy blood
- Successfully traversed Grand Line for one year
- Searched for the legendary All Blue
- Lost his leg (circumstances not yet revealed)

**Don Krieg**:
- Refuses to accept Mihawk's superior strength
- Believes logbook knowledge can overcome power gap
- Shoots own crew for protesting
- Plans to betray those who helped him
- Fleet reduced from 5,000 to 100 survivors

**Gin**:
- Second-in-command of Krieg Pirates
- Honorable despite serving evil captain
- Unaware of Krieg's betrayal plan
- Feels gratitude and apologizes to Baratie staff
