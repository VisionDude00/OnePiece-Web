## Additional Notes

### Series Context
Episode 507 is historically significant because:
- It sets up the two-year timeskip, fundamentally changing One Piece's structure
- It reveals Kuma's true allegiance, recontextualizing events from 100+ episodes prior
- It begins Luffy's Haki training arc, essential for New World survival
- The straw hat's return is one of the most emotionally resonant moments in the series

### Manga vs. Anime
- Episode 507 adapts Chapter 591, pages 2-16
- Remaining pages of Chapter 591 were adapted in Episode 510
- Anime expanded Hancock's fantasy scene with additional comedy
- Anime added more detail to Rayleigh's Calm Belt crossing

### Future Payoffs

- Episode 516 - Luffy's decision and training begins
