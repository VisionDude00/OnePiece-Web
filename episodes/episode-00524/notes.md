## Research Date
December 17, 2025

## Episode Overview
Episode 524 "Deadly Combat under the Sea! The Demon of the Ocean Strikes!" is an action-packed episode that showcases the Straw Hats' post-timeskip abilities while introducing Surume the Kraken. The episode serves as a power demonstration episode, showing how much the crew has grown during their two years apart.

## Key Findings

### Character Debuts
- **Surume (the Kraken)**: A massive 300-meter tall sea creature with 18+ tentacles. Age 120, birthday June 9th. Will later become an ally of the Straw Hats. This is his first appearance in both manga (Chapter 604) and anime (Episode 524).

### Power Reveals
The episode features multiple demonstrations of post-timeskip abilities:
1. **Robin's Mil Fleur: Gigantesco Mano** - Her most significant new technique, creating giant limbs
2. **Franky's Rocket Launchers** - New weaponry from Vegapunk's technology
3. **Chopper's improved Guard Point** - Better control over Zoan transformations
4. **Luffy's improved Gear Third** - Teased at episode end (no shrinking side effect anymore)

### Callbacks
- Momoo's return and recognition of Nami, Sanji, and Luffy from the Arlong Park arc (Episodes 31-33)
- This is a nice narrative touch showing the lasting impact of earlier adventures

### Production Notes
- Adapted parts of Chapters 604-605, which was noted as covering "more pages since Episode 404"
- Animation Director: Aya Komaki
- Screenplay: Tomohiro Nakayama
- Opening: "We Go!" by Hiroshi Kitadani (Episodes 517-590)
- No ending theme (ending themes were omitted from Episode 279 to 1070)

### Episode Pacing
Contemporary reviews from 2011 noted the episode had an "odd feeling" as it tried to find its footing with the reunited crew. The reviewer felt something was "just a little bit off" since the crew had been separated for so long in the series narrative.
