## Episode Synopsis
The Straw Hat Pirates attempt an embarrassing combination technique called Pirates Docking 6: Big Emperor to fight the giant zombie Oars, but Robin refuses to participate due to embarrassment. The crew then shifts to a brilliantly coordinated series of individual attacks that successfully topple Oars. Meanwhile, Nami discovers Thriller Bark's treasure vault is empty, Brook searches for milk and salt in the kitchen, and Bartholomew Kuma mysteriously appears on the Thousand Sunny.

## Major Events Documented

### 1. Nami's Discovery (02:30)
- Nami finds the treasure vault completely empty
- Encounters squirrels who nearly alert guards

### 2. Oars' Unique Technique (05:15)
- **Gomu Gomu no Shirimochi** debuts
- Shows Oars can create his own techniques beyond Luffy's shadow

### 3. Pirates Docking 6: Big Emperor Attempt (07:00-09:00)
- Franky initiates the combination technique
- Formation: Franky (body), Chopper (head), Usopp (right arm), Zoro & Sanji (legs)
- **Robin refuses** to be the left arm - iconic moment
- Never completed, becomes a memorable comedic scene

### 4. Coordinated Attack Strategy (10:00-16:45)
- **Usopp**: Tokuyo Abura Boshi (Oil Star) - makes Oars slip
- **Zoro**: Dai Gekken with Heavy Nunchaku - attacks left arm
- **Robin**: Cien Fleur: Big Tree - restrains right arm
- **Franky & Chopper**: Super Heavy Gong - attacks jaw
- **Sanji**: Destroys supporting leg - final blow
- **Result**: Oars falls face-first to the ground

### 5. Brook's Preparation (18:00)
- Searches kitchen for milk (bone healing) and salt (zombie purification)
- Shows strategic thinking and self-sufficiency

### 6. Kuma's Arrival (20:30)
- Bartholomew Kuma appears on the Thousand Sunny
- Seeks Gecko Moria's location
- Critical foreshadowing for episodes 368-377
