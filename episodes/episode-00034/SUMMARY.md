## Synopsis
Usopp reveals he survived his encounter with Nami, who had stabbed her own hand to spare him. Arlong's officers sink a Marine rescue ship led by Commodore Pudding Pudding. Luffy refuses to leave the island without Nami, while Nojiko begins explaining Nami's troubled past and her adoptive mother Bell-mère.
