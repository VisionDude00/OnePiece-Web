# Research Notes for Episode 240

## Episode Overview
Episode 240, "Eternal Farewell? Nico Robin: The Woman Who Draws Darkness!" is one of the most emotionally impactful episodes in the Water 7 arc. This episode marks Robin's official departure from the Straw Hat Pirates and sets up the entire Enies Lobby arc.

## Key Research Findings

### Episode Information
- **Japanese Air Date:** August 7, 2005
- **US Air Date (Funimation):** November 10, 2012
- **Manga Chapters Covered:** 339-340
- **Arc:** Water 7
- **Saga:** Water 7 Saga

### Major Events
1. **CP9 Revelation** - Kokoro tells Franky about CP9, revealing they are the true culprits behind Iceburg's attack
2. **Robin's Meeting with CP9** - Robin is shown meeting with CP9 members in secret
3. **Robin's Departure** - The pivotal scene where Robin tells Sanji and Chopper she won't return to the crew
4. **Luffy's Faith** - Luffy refuses to believe Robin would kill someone, showing unwavering trust
5. **Approaching Typhoon** - Nami warns about an incoming typhoon threatening Water 7

### Character Moments

#### Robin
- Central character of this episode
- Announces her departure from the Straw Hat Pirates
- Confirms involvement in attacking Iceburg
- Meeting with CP9 members
- Refers to herself as someone who "draws darkness"

#### Luffy
- Demonstrates absolute faith in Robin despite evidence
- Refuses to accept she would commit murder
- Shows leadership in deciding to investigate the truth

#### Sanji & Chopper
- Witness Robin's departure firsthand
- Serve as messengers to the rest of the crew
- Experience the emotional impact of Robin's announcement

#### Kokoro
- Provides crucial information about CP9
- First character to explicitly name CP9 in dialogue
- Helps shift blame away from the Straw Hats

#### Franky
- Learns about CP9 from Kokoro
- Reveals he has no home anymore
- Connected to Tom's Workers storyline

### Themes
1. **Trust and Betrayal** - Robin appears to betray the crew while Luffy maintains absolute trust
2. **Sacrifice** - Robin's actions hint at a deeper sacrifice (protecting the crew)
3. **Loyalty** - The crew's determination to find the truth rather than abandon Robin
4. **Isolation** - Robin's physical separation from Sanji/Chopper across the river symbolizes her emotional distance

### Opening/Ending Songs
- **Opening:** "Kokoro no Chizu" by BOYSTYLE (episodes 207-263)
- **Ending:** "Eternal Pose" by Asia Engineer (episodes 231-245)
- Note: "Eternal Pose" is particularly fitting as it's one of the last episodes before Robin's absence

### Animation Notes
- Episode extends several manga scenes with additional dialogue and reaction shots
- Emotional impact emphasized through animation choices
- Standard animation quality

### CP9 Information (as known by this episode)
- First explicitly named in dialogue in this episode
- Previously appeared undercover in earlier episodes
- Secret Cipher Pol organization
- Assassins trained in Rokushiki
- Members revealed by this point: Rob Lucci, Kaku, Kalifa, Blueno
- All worked undercover in Water 7 (Galley-La Company and Blueno's Bar)

### Voice Actors
- **Robin (JP):** Yuriko Yamaguchi
- **Robin (EN):** Stephanie Young (Funimation dub)
- Note: Veronica Taylor voiced Robin in the 4Kids dub
