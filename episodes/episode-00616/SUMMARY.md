# Episode 616: A Surprising Outcome! White Chase vs. Vergo!

## Basic Information
- **Episode Number**: 616
- **Japanese Title**: 衝撃の決着！白猟（スモーカー）VSヴェルゴ！ (Shōgeki no Ketchaku! Sumōkā tai Verugo!)
- **Air Date (Japan)**: October 13, 2013
- **Air Date (US Simulcast)**: October 12, 2013
- **Arc**: Punk Hazard
- **Saga**: Dressrosa Saga
- **Manga Chapter**: Primarily Chapter 690 "S.A.D."

## Episode Summary

Episode 616 is one of the most pivotal episodes of the Punk Hazard arc, featuring intense battles and a major turning point in the conflict against Doflamingo's organization.

Luffy continues his assault on Caesar Clown, ignoring Caesar's warnings about the danger of Doflamingo. Caesar demonstrates his Gastille technique, shooting beams of superheated gas, but Luffy remains undeterred.

The main focus is the brutal confrontation between Smoker and Vergo in the SAD Production Room. Vergo overwhelms Smoker with his superior Armament Haki, using his signature Oni Take technique to break Smoker's Nanashaku Jitte in half. Despite being severely beaten, Smoker makes a crucial decision that changes everything.

In a moment of tremendous character growth, Smoker uses his Logia abilities to create a smokescreen and retrieve Law's heart from Vergo, returning it to the pirate. This decision represents Smoker choosing true justice over rigid Marine doctrine, as he helps a pirate to defeat a corrupt Marine.

With his heart restored, Law regains his full power and declares to Doflamingo that the two years since the War of the Best have been a time of preparation. He proclaims that the Eleven Supernovas will lead the new era, stating "I broke the gear" of the old system.

Vergo responds by demonstrating full-body Armament Haki, coating his entire body in jet-black Haki in one of the most impressive displays of advanced Haki mastery shown up to this point in the series. However, Law's power proves superior. In a stunning display, Law uses his Room ability to cut through Vergo's Haki, bisecting him along with the entire SAD production chamber and part of Punk Hazard island itself.

This episode marks the destruction of Doflamingo's SMILE manufacturing operation and establishes Law as a major force in the New World, capable of feats that reshape the landscape both literally and figuratively.

## Key Events

1. **Caesar Warns Luffy About Doflamingo** - Caesar attempts to warn Luffy about underestimating Joker, but Luffy continues his attack.

2. **Gastille Technique Debut** - Caesar uses his Gas-Gas Fruit to shoot superheated gas beams at Luffy.

3. **Vergo Defeats Smoker** - Vergo overwhelms Smoker with superior Haki, breaking his jitte with the Oni Take technique.

4. **Smoker Retrieves Law's Heart** - In a pivotal moment of character growth, Smoker helps Law by returning his heart.

5. **Law's Declaration of the New Era** - Law announces that the Eleven Supernovas will lead the new era and that he has "broken the gear."

6. **Full-Body Armament Haki Revealed** - Vergo demonstrates advanced Haki mastery by coating his entire body.

7. **Law Defeats Vergo and Destroys SAD** - Law bisects Vergo and destroys the SAD chamber, cutting through part of Punk Hazard island.

## Character Debuts

No new characters debut in this episode.

## Technique Debuts

- **Gastille** (Caesar Clown) - Superheated gas beam attack
- **Oni Take** (Vergo) - Haki-imbued bamboo strike
- **Full-Body Armament Haki** (Vergo) - Complete body coating in Busoshoku Haki

## Major Themes

- **Generational Change**: Law's declaration establishes the Worst Generation as the force that will reshape the world
- **Justice and Morality**: Smoker's decision to help Law demonstrates that true justice transcends rigid rules
- **Power and Growth**: The episode showcases how the Supernovas have grown over the timeskip

## Significance

This episode is significant for multiple reasons:

1. **Power Scaling**: Establishes Law as capable of cutting through advanced Haki and performing island-level feats
2. **Story Progression**: Destroys Doflamingo's SAD operation, setting up future conflicts
3. **Character Development**: Shows Smoker's evolution beyond rigid Marine justice
4. **World Building**: Law's declaration about the new era sets up the larger narrative of generational change
5. **Thematic Importance**: Represents the literal and metaphorical "breaking of gears" in the established world order

## Production Notes

- **Opening Song**: "Hands Up!" by Kota Shinzato (Episodes 591-628)
- **No Ending Song**: Episode 616 did not have an ending theme (Toei stopped using endings from Episode 279-1070)
- **Director**: Takashi Ōtsuka
- **Screenplay**: Yoshiyuki Suga
- **Animation Director**: Masayuki Takagi
- **TV Rating**: 8.2% (Ranked 4th in its time slot)
- **IMDb Rating**: 9.2/10

## Fan Reception

Episode 616 is highly regarded by fans for:
- Smoker's character moment and sacrifice
- Law's epic declaration about the new era
- The stunning visual of Law's attack cutting through Vergo and the island
- Vergo's full-body Haki display
- The intense emotional weight of the battles

The episode represents a turning point not just for the Punk Hazard arc, but for the series as a whole, as it establishes the Worst Generation's role in reshaping the One Piece world.
