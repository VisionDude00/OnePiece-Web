- Introduces the benevolent ruler (Neptune) who will be threatened

- Sets up the dramatic irony of Neptune inviting the Straw Hats while danger approaches

- Cuts between peaceful Straw Hat scenes and violent Hody scenes
- Creates tension through juxtaposition

- Grand, theatrical entrance befitting a king
