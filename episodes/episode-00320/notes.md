# Research Notes for Episode 320

## Research Date
November 19, 2025

## Episode Overview
**Title:** "Everyone Finally Has A Bounty! A Pirate Group Worth Over Six Hundred Million!"
**Arc:** Post-Enies Lobby Arc
**Air Date:** August 19, 2007

## Key Findings

### Major Events
This episode is particularly significant as it marks the first time ALL Straw Hat crew members receive bounties simultaneously (though Usopp's is under his Sogeking alias). The total bounty revealed is 667,000,050 berries.

### First Mentions
- **Florian Triangle**: First mentioned in this episode by Kokoro, setting up the Thriller Bark arc
- **Fish-Man Island as Grand Line Halfway Point**: Establishes the crew's progress

### Notable Character Moments
- **Franky**: Extended flashbacks showing his transformation into a cyborg and relationship with Tom
- **Chopper**: Receives the comedically low bounty of 50 berries, starting a long-running gag
- **Sanji**: Gets a drawn wanted poster instead of a photograph (explained in Episode 321)

## Notable Observations

### Production Details
- Episode 320 achieved a 9.8% TV rating in Japan, ranking 5th for its week
- The episode only adapts pages 10-19 of Chapter 435, showing the anime's slower pacing
- No ending song (One Piece stopped airing endings from episode 279)

### Thematic Elements
The episode balances multiple tones effectively:
- **Triumphant**: The bounty reveals show the crew's growth
- **Bittersweet**: Franky must leave Water 7
- **Comedic**: Chopper's 50 berry bounty, Sanji's drawn poster
- **Nostalgic**: Tom flashbacks and legacy themes

### Fan Reception
- The bounty reveals are considered one of the most memorable moments in the Post-Enies Lobby arc
- Chopper's low bounty sparked extensive fan theories and discussions
- The Florian Triangle introduction successfully built mystery for the upcoming arc

## Final Thoughts
Episode 320 serves as an excellent transition episode between major arcs. It provides closure to the Enies Lobby consequences (bounties), sets up future adventures (Florian Triangle), and develops Franky's character arc toward joining the crew. The bounty reveals are handled with a good mix of drama and comedy, making it a memorable episode despite being relatively low-action.
