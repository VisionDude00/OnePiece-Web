## Plot Summary
Usopp engages in a desperate battle against Perona and her overwhelming Horo Horo no Mi abilities. Despite facing her intangible astral form, size manipulation, and explosive ghost attacks, Usopp uses his observational skills and intelligence to deduce her critical weakness. He discovers that while Perona's astral projection is invincible, her physical body lies defenseless in a hidden room. After calling upon his Sogeking persona to purify Kumashi with salt, Usopp locates Perona's room and destroys the door with his Atlas Suisei technique, setting up her defeat. Meanwhile, Luffy continues his intense battle against Gecko Moria, countering the Warlord's shadow manipulation with creative rubber techniques.
