# Research Notes for Episode 362

## Episode Overview
Episode 362 "Slashes Dancing on the Roof! Finale - Zoro vs. Ryuma" is a pivotal episode in the Thriller Bark arc, featuring one of Zoro's most significant battles and the acquisition of the legendary sword Shusui.

## Key Research Findings

### Main Plot Points
1. **Zoro vs Ryuma Duel**: The battle begins in Hogback's laboratory and concludes on the mansion roof
2. **Hiryu: Kaen Debut**: First appearance of this iconic technique
3. **Shusui Acquisition**: Zoro receives one of the 21 Great Grade swords
4. **Brook's Shadow Returns**: Major character development moment for Brook

### Production Details
- Aired July 13, 2008 in Japan
- TV Rating: 7.8% (ranked 6th that week)
- Adapted Chapters 466-467 of the manga
- Notable animation anomaly: Zoro eyecatcher missing theme audio

### Character Significance
**Zoro**: Major character development showing his growth as a swordsman
**Ryuma (Zombie)**: Final appearance - body destroyed at end of episode
**Brook**: Shadow restoration is crucial for his eventual joining of the crew

### Thematic Connections
- **Episode 1017**: Hiryu: Kaen used again against Kaido, proving long-term significance
- **Episode 937**: Zoro returns Shusui to Wano, showing character growth
- **Connection to "Monsters"**: Oda's earlier one-shot featuring Ryuma's dragon-slaying

### Cultural References
- Ryuma's techniques use French fencing terminology mixed with musical terms
