### Monster Time Story
- Takes place in Monston (Monster Town), a peaceful community of mythological creatures
- Features parody versions of the crew: Lugyao (dragon), Zoromilk (minotaur), Stranami (mermaid), Ussopaka (centaur), Saneara (kappa), Chopaken (kraken), and Rodusa (gorgon)
- Plot centers on Rodusa losing her earrings and her friends searching for replacement treasures
- Ends with a bittersweet comedic moment where Rodusa accidentally petrifies all her friends
