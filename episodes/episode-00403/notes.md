## Important Observations

### 1. **Haki Foreshadowing**
Episode 403 is one of the earliest clear demonstrations of Armament Haki in the anime (through Sentomaru's Ashigara Dokkoi), though Haki isn't formally explained until much later. This is significant for the series' power system development.

### 2. **Anime-Original Extensions**
The anime significantly expanded the Kid and Law Pirates' battle against the Pacifista compared to the manga, showing more of the Supernova pirates' struggles during the Sabaody chaos.

### 3. **Sentomaru's Character**
Interesting character quirk: Sentomaru claims to have "the tightest lips in the world" while simultaneously revealing classified information about Pacifistas and Vegapunk. This ironic trait becomes a recurring joke.

### 4. **Funimation Simulcast**
This episode was notable in production history as it was planned to be Funimation's first simulcast episode, but the release was delayed due to an early leak.

### 5. **Character Design**
Sentomaru's design is based on Kintarō (Golden Boy), a legendary Japanese folk hero raised on Mount Ashigara - directly referenced in his signature attack name "Ashigara Dokkoi."

## Fan Theory Validation

### Confirmed Theories
1. **Sentomaru's Loyalty to Vegapunk** - Fans correctly predicted from this episode that Sentomaru's primary loyalty was to Vegapunk rather than the World Government, which was confirmed during the Egghead arc when he betrayed the Marines to protect Vegapunk.

2. **Pacifista Technology Evolution** - Fan speculation that the Pacifistas were just the first generation of cyborg technology was confirmed with the later introduction of the Seraphim.

### Ongoing Theories
1. **Zoro vs Kizaru Rematch** - Strong fan theory about an eventual rematch between Zoro and Kizaru, drawing parallels to Rayleigh's role. While Luffy fought Kizaru in Egghead instead, this theory remains popular in the community.
