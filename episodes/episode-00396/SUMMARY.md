## Synopsis
At the Human Auction House, Saint Charlos places an exorbitant 500 million berry bid for Camie, far exceeding the Straw Hats' funds. When Luffy crashes through the building, Hatchan accidentally reveals his fish-man identity while trying to stop Luffy. After Charlos shoots Hatchan and mocks him, Luffy punches the Celestial Dragon in the face, triggering a massive incident that will bring an Admiral to the archipelago.

## Significance
This is one of the most iconic episodes in One Piece history, featuring Luffy's legendary punch of Saint Charlos - an act that:
- Challenged the World Government's absolute authority
- Triggered the Celestial Dragon Hit-and-Run Incident
- Led to Admiral Kizaru's deployment
- Directly caused the Straw Hats' separation and two-year timeskip
- Influenced Bartholomew Kuma's belief in Luffy's potential
