# Research Notes for Episode 381

## Episode Overview
Episode 381 "A New Crewmate! The Musician, Humming Brook" is the finale of the Thriller Bark Arc and marks several significant moments in One Piece.

## Key Research Findings

### 1. Brook's Official Joining
- Brook becomes the 9th member of the Straw Hat Pirates
- His former bounty of 33 million Berries is revealed
- Brook's eyecatcher debuts in this episode
- This completes the pre-timeskip crew roster

### 2. Critical Foreshadowing
- **Ace's Vivre Card burning** - One of the most important foreshadowing moments in the series
- Sets up the Impel Down and Marineford arcs
- Luffy's decision NOT to rescue Ace shows his trust but later becomes significant

### 3. Lola's Vivre Card
- Lola gives Nami her mother's Vivre Card
- Mother later revealed to be Big Mom (Charlotte Linlin)
- This becomes crucial in the Whole Cake Island arc (episodes 825+)
- Lola is unaware her mother holds a grudge against her

### 4. Zoro's Symbolic Gesture
- Zoro plants his broken sword Yubashiri at the Rumbar Pirates' memorial
- Shows respect for Brook's loss and the fallen warriors
- Yubashiri was destroyed in Episode 271 by Captain Shu
- Zoro's eyecatcher updated to show Shusui
