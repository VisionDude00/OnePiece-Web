## Synopsis
Boa Hancock returns to Amazon Lily and refuses Elder Nyon's plea to answer a World Government summons. While bathing, Luffy accidentally crashes into the bathhouse and sees a mysterious mark on Hancock's back - the Hoof of the Soaring Dragon slave brand. Despite Hancock's attempts to petrify him with her beauty, Luffy remains completely unaffected, shocking her. Hancock orders his immediate execution to protect her secret.

## Major Plot Points

### The World Government Summons
Elder Nyon warns that Hancock must answer the government's call to maintain her Warlord status and protect Amazon Lily. Hancock's refusal puts the entire island at risk.

### The Gorgon Legend
The Kuja people believe the three sisters defeated a Gorgon monster and were cursed with petrifying eyes on their backs. This fabricated legend conceals their true past as Celestial Dragon slaves.

### Luffy's Discovery
Luffy becomes the first man outside the Gorgon Sisters to see the slave mark on Hancock's back, violating her most closely guarded secret and leading to his execution order.

### Immunity to Beauty
Luffy's complete indifference to Hancock's beauty shocks her, as he becomes the first person ever unaffected by her appearance. This plants the seed for their unique relationship.
