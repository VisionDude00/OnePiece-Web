# Research Notes for Episode 168

## Episode Overview
Episode 168: "The Python Strikes! The Survival Game Begins!" is a pivotal episode in the Skypiea Arc that introduces Nola and sets up the Survival Game conflict structure.

## Key Research Findings

### Historical Significance
- **Last Episode with Original Themes**: Episode 168 is the final episode to feature both "Hikari e" as the opening and "FAITH" as the ending theme, marking a transition point in the anime's music.
- **Manga Correspondence**: Adapts Chapters 255-256 of the manga.

### Character Debuts
Three characters debut in this episode:
1. **Nola** - A 400+ year old giant python with deep connections to the Noland-Kalgara backstory
2. **Shandia Chief** - The unnamed leader of the Shandia tribe (notable for being the only character with a Vivre Card to remain unnamed)
3. **Isa** - Aisa's mother, representing the civilian/family aspect of the Shandia

### Thematic Elements
This episode heavily emphasizes:
- **Inherited Will**: The 400-year conflict continues through generations
- **Displacement and Homeland**: The Shandia's fight to reclaim Upper Yard
- **Living History**: Nola serves as a living connection to events 400 years ago
