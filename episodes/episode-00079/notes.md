# Research Notes for Episode 79

## Episode Overview
Episode 79 "A Raid! The Tin Tyrant and Tin Plate Wapol!" marks the beginning of the Drum Island Arc proper, introducing major antagonist Wapol and ally Dalton.

## Key Research Findings

### Character Debuts
- **Wapol**: Former king of Drum Kingdom, now pirate captain with Baku Baku no Mi
- **Dalton**: Former Royal Guard captain, now protector of Drum Island with Ushi Ushi no Mi Model Bison (not shown in this episode)
- **Chess**: Minister of Defense, skilled archer
- **Kuromarimo**: Magistrate with afro hair manipulation abilities

### Significant Moments
1. **Bliking Submarine**: First submarine ship in One Piece series
2. **Going Merry Damaged**: Wapol eats part of the ship's railing
3. **Vivi's Sacrifice**: Takes bullet to prevent violence, key diplomatic moment
4. **Grand Line Climate**: Vivi explains the four island climate types

### Production Notes
- Akemi Okamura returns as Nami's VA after 9-episode maternity leave (episodes 70-78)
- Episode condenses content from manga chapters 127 (p.19), 128 (p.1, 4-6), 130 (p.4-19), 131 (p.2-19), and 132 (p.2-15)
- Chapter 129 completely skipped in adaptation

### Voice Acting
- Japanese: Bin Shimada (Wapol), Kenichi Ono (Dalton)
- English: Andy Mullins (Wapol), J. Michael Tatum (Dalton)

### World Building
- Introduction of Winter Island climate type
- First submarine vessel (Bliking)
- Drum Island's traumatic history hinted at
- Island's distrust of pirates established

### Thematic Elements
- Sacrifice and compassion (Vivi taking the bullet)
- Loyalty and protection (Luffy defending the Going Merry)
- Trauma and healing (Drum Island's fear of pirates)
- Diplomacy vs violence
