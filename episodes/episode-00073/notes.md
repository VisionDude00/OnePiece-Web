### Episode Significance
Episode 73 is a pivotal episode in the Little Garden arc that shows the tragic corruption of Dorry and Brogy's century-long sacred duel by Baroque Works. The episode is emotionally powerful, showcasing:

1. **Dorry's unwavering honor** - Despite severe injuries, he insists on fighting
2. **Brogy's emotional depth** - His tears over defeating his friend show their bond
3. **Mr. 3's cunning villainy** - His systematic sabotage and artistic cruelty
4. **The stakes for the Straw Hats** - Multiple crew members trapped, Vivi captured
