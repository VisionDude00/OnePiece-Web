### Key Findings

#### 1. Thousand Sunny Naming
- The naming scene is a major celebratory moment for the crew
- Luffy suggests ridiculous names like "Dumpling-Gorilla-Lion"
- Iceburg ultimately suggests "Thousand Sunny" which the crew accepts
- This marks the official christening of their second ship

#### 2. Coup de Burst Debut
- First use of the Thousand Sunny's emergency acceleration system
- Uses exactly 3 barrels of cola
- Launches ship 1 kilometer into the air
- Successfully escapes Vice Admiral Garp's attack
- Becomes a recurring escape mechanism throughout the series

#### 3. Post-Enies Lobby Bounties
All crew members receive updated or first-time bounties:
- Luffy: 300,000,000 (tripled from 100,000,000)
- Zoro: 120,000,000 (doubled from 60,000,000)
- Robin: 80,000,000 (unchanged, photo updated)
- Sanji: 77,000,000 (first bounty, poor sketch)
- Franky: 44,000,000 (first bounty)
- Sogeking/Usopp: 30,000,000 (first bounty)
- Nami: 16,000,000 (first bounty)
- Chopper: 50 berries (mistaken for pet)

Total crew bounty: 667,000,050 berries

#### 4. Worldwide Reactions
The episode features reactions from multiple locations:
- **Foosha Village:** Villagers celebrate, Makino loves the crew, Woop Slap disapproves
- **Syrup Village:** Kaya and Usopp Pirates recognize Sogeking as Usopp
- **Shimotsuki Village:** Zoro's sensei proud but advises students not to follow his path
- **Cocoyashi Village:** Genzo objects to Nami's "lewd" poster, Nojiko sees her happiness
- **Drum Island:** Kureha pleased to see Chopper doing well
- **Baratie:** Zeff and chefs mock Sanji's sketch but display it proudly
- **Arabasta:** Vivi trusts Luffy regarding Robin
- **Baltigo:** Dragon sees Luffy's bounty
- **Grand Line:** Smoker vows to defeat the Straw Hats in New World

#### 5. Revolutionary Army Introduction
- First appearance of Baltigo (Revolutionary Army HQ)
- Terry Gilteo debuts as intelligence officer
- Dragon's second appearance after Episode 53 (Loguetown)
- Shows Dragon's awareness and approval of Luffy's path
- Hints at Revolutionary Army's interest in Straw Hats' actions

## Conclusion
Episode 324 is a pivotal transitional episode that caps off the Water 7 Saga while setting up future storylines. The worldwide reaction montage effectively demonstrates the Straw Hats' growing influence, while the Baltigo scenes plant important seeds for the Revolutionary Army subplot. The Thousand Sunny's christening and Coup de Burst debut establish the ship as a character in its own right with capabilities that will be crucial going forward.
