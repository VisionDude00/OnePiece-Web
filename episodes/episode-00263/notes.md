# Research Notes for Episode 263

## Episode Overview
**Title:** The Judicial Island! Full View of Enies Lobby!
**Japanese:** 司法の島!エニエス・ロビーの全貌! (Shihō no shima! Eniesu Robī no zenbō!)
**Air Date:** April 30, 2006
**Arc:** Water 7 Arc (Final Episode)

## Key Research Findings

### Major Significance
- **Final Episode of Water 7 Arc**: This episode marks the end of the 35-episode Water 7 Arc (episodes 229-263)
- **First Glimpse of Robin's Past**: This is the first time the anime explicitly shows Robin's traumatic past involving the Buster Call on Ohara
- **Enies Lobby Debut**: The Judicial Island makes its first appearance, setting up the next major arc
- **Voice Acting Milestone**: Last episode featuring Kazue Ikura as Chopper's voice (she temporarily replaced Ikue Ōtani during episodes 254-263)

### Thematic Elements
The episode heavily focuses on:
1. **Robin's Sacrifice**: Her willingness to protect her crewmates despite believing she endangers them
2. **The Weight of the Past**: First introduction of the Buster Call trauma that has defined Robin's life
3. **Loyalty and Determination**: Sogeking's brave stand articulating that Robin belongs to the crew

### Music Significance
- Final use of opening "Kokoro no Chizu" by BOYSTYLE (episodes 207-263)
- Final use of ending "Asu wa Kuru Kara" by Tohoshinki/TVXQ (episodes 256-263)
- Both songs mark the end of an era before the Enies Lobby Arc begins

## Additional Observations

### Narrative Importance
Episode 263 serves as a crucial transition point:
- Ends the Water 7 investigation/mystery arc
- Begins the rescue mission arc at Enies Lobby
- Shifts from infiltration to open assault
- Transforms Robin from fugitive to prisoner to be rescued

This episode is emotionally significant because:
- First time viewers understand why Robin left
- Sogeking's declaration emphasizes crew bonds
- Robin's protection of Sanji/Sogeking shows her internal conflict
- Sets up the emotional payoff of "I Want to Live!" moment in later episodes
