## Key Findings

### Historical Significance
This episode is historically significant for being the **first time in the anime** that Devil Fruit reincarnation is shown on-screen. While the concept had been theorized by fans, episode 602 provided visual confirmation of how Devil Fruits reincarnate when their user dies.

### Devil Fruit Reincarnation Mechanics
The Sara Sara no Mi, Model: Axolotl reincarnated into an apple that was nearby on a sled, suggesting:
- Devil Fruits may reincarnate into the nearest compatible fruit
- The transformation happens immediately upon the user's death
- Caesar appears to have known about this mechanic (strategically placed apples)
