# Episode 513 Research Summary

## Episode Information
**Title:** Pirates Get On The Move! Astounding New World!
**Japanese Title:** 動き出す海賊たち！驚天動地の新世界 (Ugokidasu Kaizoku-tachi! Kyōtendōchi no Shinsekai)
**Air Date:** September 4, 2011 (Japan) / September 3, 2011 (US Simulcast)
**Arc:** Post-War Arc
**Saga:** Summit War Saga
**Manga Chapter:** 595 (pages 3-15)

## Synopsis
Episode 513 showcases the various Supernova pirate crews making their moves into the New World after the war at Marineford. The episode follows multiple storylines: Trafalgar Law and the Heart Pirates discuss their journey near Sabaody Archipelago; X Drake deliberately provokes Kaido's forces on a winter island; Scratchmen Apoo battles giant boars and discovers invisible air pathways; Jewelry Bonney is defeated by the Blackbeard Pirates and subsequently captured by Admiral Akainu, who reveals her mysterious connection to the World Government; and Tony Tony Chopper returns to Birdie Kingdom to continue his medical training.

4. **Apoo Battles Giant Boars** - Scratchmen Apoo fights enormous wild boars

6. **Bonney Defeated by Blackbeard** - Blackbeard Pirates defeat the Bonney Pirates
7. **Akainu Arrives** - Admiral Akainu's arrival causes Blackbeard to flee

9. **Chopper's Failed Disguise** - Chopper tries "Chopper Mask" but is instantly recognized

## Research Notes
- Episode 513 was simulcast in the US on September 3, 2011, one day before the Japanese broadcast
- This is part of the Post-War arc showing what various characters are doing after Marineford
- The Bonney mystery revealed here won't be fully explained for many years, making it one of the series' longest-running plot threads
- Scotch's appearance is the first glimpse of Kaido's operations in the New World, years before the Wano arc
- The episode adapts the first part of Chapter 595; Episode 514 adapts the remainder
