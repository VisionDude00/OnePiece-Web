# Research Notes for Episode 517

## Episode Overview
Episode 517 "A New Chapter Begins - The Straw Hat Crew Reunites!" is a landmark episode marking the beginning of the post-timeskip era and the second half of One Piece. It aired on October 2, 2011, in Japan.

## Research Process

### Sources Consulted
- One Piece Wiki (Fandom) - Primary source for episode details
- Wikipedia episode listing

### Key Findings

#### Major Milestone
This episode represents one of the most significant turning points in One Piece:
- First episode of the series' second half (after 516 episodes)
- Introduction of post-timeskip character designs
- New opening theme "We Go!" debuts
- New opening narration
- First use of third set of eyecatchers

#### Episode Content
The episode primarily shows:
1. Luffy's departure from Ruskaina after completing training
2. Individual arrivals of Straw Hat members at Sabaody
3. Introduction of Fake Straw Hat Crew as antagonists
4. Setup for upcoming confrontation

#### Manga Adaptation
- Adapts Chapter 598 (partially - pages 1-3, 6-16, 19, 23-24)
- Remaining content appears in Episode 518
- Several anime additions and changes noted

### Character Debuts
**New Characters:**
- Demaro Black (Fake Luffy) - Main antagonist
- Caribou - Rookie pirate with 210 million bounty
- Coribou - Caribou's brother with 190 million bounty
- Eight Fake Straw Hat crew members

**Post-Timeskip Design Debuts:**
All nine Straw Hat members show their new designs:
- Zoro: Scarred eye, more muscular
- Nami: Longer hair, more mature appearance
- Usopp: Muscular build, beard
- Sanji: Beard, hair covering both eyes
- Robin: Mature look with sunglasses
- Franky: Dramatically enhanced cyborg body
- Chopper: Smaller, cuter appearance
- Brook: Celebrity rock star look

## Additional Notes

This episode is primarily a setup episode that:
1. Reintroduces all main characters with new designs
2. Establishes the Fake Straw Hat conflict
3. Sets up the reunion that will complete in following episodes

The episode itself is light on action but heavy on character moments and setup. Most significant events will pay off in Episodes 518-522.

The absence of an ending theme (removed since Episode 279) was confirmed - the anime didn't use ending themes from 2006-2023.
