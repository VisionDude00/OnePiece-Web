## Character Debuts in This Episode
All five characters are anime-only and part of the Accino Family:
1. **Hockera** - Third son, hockey-themed fighter
2. **Salchow** - Married to Arbell, figure skating-themed fighter
3. **Arbell** - Eldest daughter of Don Accino, figure skating-themed fighter
4. **Brindo** - Twin brother with magnetic abilities
5. **Lil** - Youngest daughter, deceptive and controls plants/flying fish

## Notable Details

### Ice Sports Theme
The Accino Family members are all themed after winter/ice sports:
- Hockera: Hockey
- Salchow & Arbell: Figure skating (Salchow is even named after a real skating jump)
- Don Accino: Ironically uses heat powers despite the ice theme

### Phoenix Pirates Alliance
The Phoenix Pirates continue their temporary alliance with the Straw Hats, fighting alongside Luffy and Chopper against Brindo.

### Domo Penguins
Interesting mechanism revealed: The seemingly random iceberg movements are actually controlled by Domo Penguins pushing them from underwater, allowing the Accino Family to trap ships.

### Lovely Land Discovery
Zoro's discovery of Lovely Land is a major turning point, revealing:
- Golden castle with Art Nouveau architecture
- Tropical environment (despite being in frozen Hyokaido)
- Created by Don Accino's heat powers
- Houses his pirate flag collection

## Arc Context
Ice Hunter Arc (Episodes 326-335):
- Eighth filler arc in One Piece
- One of the longest filler arcs at 10 episodes
- Generally receives negative to mixed reviews from fans
- Takes place between Enies Lobby and Thriller Bark in the timeline
- Completely anime-original, does not exist in manga
