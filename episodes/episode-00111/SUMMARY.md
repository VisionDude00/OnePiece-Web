# Episode 111: Dash For a Miracle! Alabasta Animal Land!

**Japanese Title:** 奇跡への疾走！アラバスタ動物ランド
**Romanized:** Kiseki e no Shissō! Arabasuta Dōbutsu Rando
**Air Date:** May 12, 2002
**Arc:** Arabasta Arc
**Manga Chapters:** 179-180

---

## Quick Summary

Luffy survives his second brutal defeat by Crocodile after being stabbed and thrown into quicksand. Nico Robin mysteriously saves him and asks the cryptic question: "Why do you people with the D in your name keep on fighting?" Meanwhile, the rest of the Straw Hats race toward Alubarna on various animal companions, aided by the Kung-Fu Dugongs and Super Spot-Billed Duck Troops.

---

## Major Events

1. **Crocodile's Victory** - Crocodile stabs Luffy through the abdomen and throws him into quicksand using Desert Girasole, seemingly killing him.

2. **The Will of D Question** ⭐ **CRITICAL LORE MOMENT** - Nico Robin saves Luffy from quicksand and asks him about the "Will of D," one of the earliest and most important mentions of this central mystery in the series.

3. **Robin's Conflicted Loyalty** - Despite being Crocodile's partner, Robin saves Luffy's life, demonstrating her growing conflict and foreshadowing her eventual betrayal of Baroque Works.

4. **Animal Allies** - The Straw Hats receive help from multiple animal companions: Hasami the giant crab, Kung-Fu Dugongs (who remained loyal to Luffy), and the Super Spot-Billed Duck Troops.

5. **King Cobra Kidnapped** - It's revealed that Mr. 4 and Miss Merry Christmas have kidnapped King Cobra as part of Crocodile's conspiracy.

6. **Pell's Dedication** - Pell arrives despite severe injuries to protect the royal family, showing unwavering loyalty to Arabasta.

## Why This Episode Matters

**Historical Significance:**
This episode contains one of the most important lore moments in the entire One Piece series. Robin's question about the Will of D plants seeds for mysteries that won't be fully explored for hundreds of episodes. This single question has spawned countless fan theories and discussions over the years.

**Character Development:**
Robin's decision to save Luffy is a pivotal moment showing her conflicted loyalties and hinting at her eventual alliance with the Straw Hats. This scene establishes the foundation of their future relationship.

**Plot Progression:**
The revelation of King Cobra's kidnapping and the crew's race to Alubarna sets up the climactic battles to come. The water bottle detail is clever foreshadowing for Luffy's eventual victory strategy.
