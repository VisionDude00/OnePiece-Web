# Research Notes for Episode 165

## Episode Overview
Episode 165 "Jaya, City of Gold in the Sky! Head for God's Shrine!" is a pivotal episode in the Skypiea arc that brings together all the mysteries established during the Jaya arc. It aired on August 24, 2003 in Japan and March 1, 2008 in the US.

## Key Research Findings

### Major Revelations
1. **Jaya Rose to the Sky**: The episode reveals that the island of Jaya didn't sink 400 years ago but was launched into the sky by a Knock Up Stream. This validates Mont Blanc Noland's story completely.

2. **City of Gold Discovered**: The Straw Hats piece together that the legendary City of Gold (Shandora) is real and located on Upper Yard. By combining maps, they discover it's in the "skull's right eye" position.

3. **Mantra Introduction**: The crew discusses the mysterious Mantra ability, which will later be revealed as Observation Haki.

### Character Moments
- **Nami** takes center stage as the navigator, explaining the geographical mystery and locating the City of Gold
- **Wyper** establishes himself as a major antagonist, declaring his intent to kill Enel
- **Luffy** shows leadership by safely guiding his group through challenges and challenging Wyper

### Production Details
- Based on Chapter 252 "Junction"
- Opening: "Hikari e" by The Babystars
- Ending: "FAITH" by Ruppina
- Animation Director: Masayuki Takagi
- Writer: Junki Takegami
- TV Rating: 10.7% (Rank 5)

### Animation Notes
- One animation error noted: Luffy's chin appears uncolored in one scene
- The campfire scene was extended compared to the manga
- The Milky Road journey scene was also expanded

## Episode Significance

This episode represents a major turning point in the Skypiea arc. It's where all the puzzle pieces come together:
- The mystery of Jaya is solved
- The City of Gold is confirmed to exist
- The Shandia's motivation becomes clear
