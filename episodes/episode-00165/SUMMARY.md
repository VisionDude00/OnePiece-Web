## Key Revelations

### World-Building
- **Jaya's True Fate:** The island didn't sink but was launched into the sky 400 years ago by a Knock Up Stream
- **City of Gold Location:** Shandora exists on Upper Yard, located in the "skull's right eye" when Jaya and Skypiea maps are combined
- **Mantra:** Introduction of this mysterious power (later revealed as Observation Haki)

### Character Development
- **Nami:** Demonstrates her expertise as navigator by solving the 400-year-old mystery
- **Wyper:** Establishes his goal to defeat Enel and reclaim Upper Yard for the Shandia
- **Crew Unity:** The reunion shows the crew's resilience and ability to share knowledge

### Story Progression
- Connects the Jaya arc (episodes 141-152) with the Skypiea arc
- Validates Mont Blanc Noland's story after 400 years
- Sets up the final conflict over Upper Yard/Shandora

The revelation that Jaya rose to the sky is one of One Piece's most satisfying mysteries to solve, connecting geography, history, and character motivations in a single episode.
