## Research Summary
This episode contains one of the most important revelations in the Skypiea arc - the discovery that Upper Yard was once part of Jaya. This connects the Jaya arc to the Skypiea arc and validates Mont Blanc Noland's story after 400 years of him being branded a liar.

## Key Discoveries

### Major Plot Revelation
- **Upper Yard = Part of Jaya**: Nami, Zoro, and Robin discover that Upper Yard is the part of Jaya that was shot into the sky 400 years ago by the Knock Up Stream. This is the same island Mont Blanc Noland visited and documented.
- **Noland's House**: Physical evidence found - Nami identifies a building as the other half of Noland's house.
- **Significance**: This discovery vindicates Noland's legacy and proves Cricket's faith in his ancestor was justified.

### Character Developments
- **Wyper**: Featured prominently as he leads the Shandia warriors in planning and executing their assault on Upper Yard

- **Robin**: Personal moment where she realizes her dream of finding ancient historical truths is within reach
- **Gan Fall**: Revealed to be the former God of Skypiea, not just a knight

### Battle Progression

- Strategic planning shown with Wyper analyzing Gan Fall's motivations
- Aisa's intelligence about two fallen warriors triggers the attack
